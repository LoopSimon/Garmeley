<p>A Meadi Patrician and the co-owner of the Eastcorner Bathhouse. He is in constant contact with the surrounding areas and cities and has deals with the dwarfs of the mountains and even the Paper Cities. He prefers a soft touch as well and is supported by a variety of the richer Patricians in the city. Known for his easy smile, voluptuous shape and soft touch, Gislif has become one of the richest citizens of Niedrest, being in bed both with local and foreign criminals and merchants as well as the ruling Patrician elite and city guards.</p>
<p>During the <a href="Niedrest Incident" class="internal-link">Incident</a> of 923PA, Gislif proved himself a surprisingly heroic figure when his Bathhouse came under assault of presumably the same group of criminals responsibly for the assault on the kherovan delegation. Mellwing personally defended his defenceless customers and staff and chased the dangerous scoundrels out of his place of business, even though a foreigner with a dodgy reputation this feat earned Gislif the status of a local legend with both poor and rich citizens alike. </p>
