<p>Certainly old even for a dwarf, Morlin Oxader is the regional Headmaster of the <a href="Xerental" class="internal-link">Xerental</a> in far <a href="Nostroya" class="internal-link">Nostroya</a>, responsible for the Guilds business with private enterprises in Grachy as well as national contracts for Grachy and Ukiolla. His active role within the organization even in contrast to his elderly stature is owed to a long history of successful business acumen and a willingness to endure eccentric clients and sign a deal no matter what.</p>
<p>Usually shadowed by his personal bodyguard Anja, Morlin is a balding dwarf with a grandfatherly smile, rich and luxurious tastes in clothing and a love for pipe weed, almost perpetually wreathed in a cloud of fragrant smoke. Oxader was part of <a href="Beryl Lenkov" class="internal-link">Boyar Beryl Lenkovs</a> Hunting Party in May of 927PA during which the latter tragically passed away.</p></p>
