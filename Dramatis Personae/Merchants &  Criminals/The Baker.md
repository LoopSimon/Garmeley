<p>The figure only known as ”The Baker” controls the flow of Drugs across the entire Gulf of Butera and can be seen as the closest to a Ruler to the  crime ridden City of Ligoggia. He is in exchange with criminal elements in Battvia, Niedrest, Ogul-Vosko and even Meadrath and responsible for the shipping and creation of most of the worlds <a href="Battvian Flour" class="internal-link">Battvian Flour</a>. His nickname stems not only from embracing baking motives with his gang and practices early on in his career, but also the practice of killing those that cross his path by grinding them up under a large millstone.</p>
<p>Often viewed as a "necessary evil" by the worldly rulers and authorities around him, he has made as many enemies as he has friends and controls his domain with a mixture of Fear, Coin and Murder. He is rumoured to be the main contact of the urien Criminal known as <a href="Pigeye" class="internal-link">"Pigeye"</a> across the Kortopis.</p>
