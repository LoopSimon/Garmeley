<p>Garmogol Ilwik is the current Annod of the Ottian Syndicate and was previously the Minister for Foreign Relations in the Twin Kingdoms Council from 891-910PA. As Annod he is in charge of business directives, international relations and Syndicate Outposts on the entire Continent and sits on the Board of Directives as one of the three major Seats. He is undisputedly one of the most influential Gnomes alive and known for his coldblooded business acumen and utilitarian approach to politics.</p>
