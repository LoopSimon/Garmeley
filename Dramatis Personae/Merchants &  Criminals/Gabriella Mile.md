<p>The unknowable and mysterious Gabriella Mile is the proprietor of Pireggi & Mile, the largest Auction House in <a href="Battvia" class="internal-link">Battvia</a>. Her reputation spreads throughout Cevolia, Meadland and Urland as do her agents and influence. Although an avid socialite and desired guest, essentially nothing is known about her past or road to power and weath.</p>
