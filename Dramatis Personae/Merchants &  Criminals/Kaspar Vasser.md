<p>Kasper Vasser is the enterprising and shrewd head of the Vasser Cartel, a private enterprise operating in northern Behera formed from the remnants of Urien and Meadi Mercenary Companies. Vasser is a rogue-ish character with a silver tongue, enjoying life and death equally, with a reputation for flair and appropriate cruelty. He was born in Zeeport, northern Meadrath but was forced to leave his home for undisclosed reasons, leading him as far south as the Deserts of Behera which he calls home now. He has become incredibly over the last decade for less than clear reasons, dealing far above the station of a regular mercenary captain Vasser seems to be running his own operations now and has been rumoured to be involved in the extremely lucrative drug trade.</p>
