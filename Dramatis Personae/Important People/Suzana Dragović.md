<p>Suzana Dragović, later known as Suzana the cruel, was the first Empress of the <a href="Zepria" class="internal-link">Lands of Aman</a> and successfully united the Gorati peoples under a single throne. Born as the Queen of the small Kingdom of Zepria her conquests saw many warlords bow before her, including Kobold Khans and Shirin Generals. To this day she is a symbol of Gorati stubbornness and strength.</p>
<p>She openly embraced the moniker "the cruel" and judged dissenters and opposition without mercy, infamously wiping out the entire bloodline of the House Dajateš to the last son for their betrayal at the battle of Hastermon. Nevertheless she proved a lifelong devout follower of <a href="Zuhurism" class="internal-link">Pen-Zuhur</a> and held the Gods name in high honours.</p>
