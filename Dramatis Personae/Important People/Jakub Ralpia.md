<p>Jakub zi Ralpia is the second son of <a href="Rulers of Vihr" class="internal-link">Ras Miskomir IV of Vihr</a>, the younger brother of Vasczo zi Ralpia and a Kis or "Prince" of Vihr, as his brother remains childless he is 2nd in line to the throne. Jakub is a successful military commander and tournament knight, as well as a Captain of the famed knightly Order of the Unicorn. Following the events of the <a href="Niedrest" class="internal-link">Niedrest Incident of 923PA</a> and the start of the <a href="Garmian Succession Crisis" class="internal-link">Garmish Civil War</a>, he participated in the fighting against the wishes of his father on the side of <a href="Coster of Melmbirth" class="internal-link">Count Coster of Melmbirth</a> and the Green Banner League to uphold gethric values and smite southern heathens. His participation in the raids of eastern Garmeley alongside the Kol river have made him an infamous commander on both sides of the battlefield, his eagerness to join the fighting at all is often cited as a reason for the international escalation of the conflict and the worrying threat to draw in the Vihri Kingdom itself.</p>
<p>He participated in the Battle of Jestrick Fields and slew Count Miklas of Koltree on the battlefield but was later forced to retreat as the engagement ended up as an overall devastating loss for the northern forces. His terror of eastern Garmeley continued afterwards with Jakub continuously inviting knights and mercenaries from across the Trollbacks to join his cause; while many answered and fought in his campaign, equally as many soldiers of fortune quickly turned to looting brigands and even further destroyed the garmian countryside.</p>
