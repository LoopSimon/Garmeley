<p>A living Legend in Cevolia and beyond, the current Great Master of the Fellowship of the Dancing Flame is a Knight without repute, a fighter of immense prowess and a politician of significant influence. Known throughout the region as the Capellianci or literally "White-Hair" for the flowing hairs of the Nereid Queen Igista he is said to have slain he is one of the most well-known mortals in all of <a href="Cevolia" class="internal-link">Cevolia</a>.</p>
