<p>The Count of Forrington was one of the most powerful and rich nobles during the reign of Duchess Agnes of Garmeley his late aunt. After her death at the Midsummer Festival of 922PA he was left as the heir-appearent to the Riverthrone and became the leader of the Southern Garmish Alliance opposed by the Green Banner League declared by Coster the Count of Melmbirth. War later broke out between the southern and northern factions when Hyneks younger Sister Regio was captured by agents of the Green Banner.</p>
<p>Proving himself a clever military leader and magnetic figurehead, Hyneks cause was largely succesful in the early years of the War, further discussed <a href="Garmian Succession Crisis" class="internal-link">here</a>, likely one of the reasons why he found himself at the wrong end of an assassins blade in the february of 927PA while leading the Siege on Redflow, the Capital of Garmeley.</p>
