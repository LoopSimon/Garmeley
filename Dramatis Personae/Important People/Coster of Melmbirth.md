<p>The Leader of the northern Green Banner League and Count of Melmbirth, Coster Zrizk o Melm has found himself as the leading figure of a rapidly expanding <a href="Garmian Succession Crisis" class="internal-link">conflict</a>. What started as a play for power and to undermine and break southern dominance within the Duchy, has developed into a large scale international conflict with more and more deaths and participants. Costers kidnapping of Regia of Forrington inarguably caused war to break out between the Green Banner and the Garmian Alliance, combined with the ruthless pillaging and destruction of eastern Garmeley and the rumoured Assassination of <a href="Hynek of Forrington" class="internal-link">Hynek of Forrington</a>, Coster has been dubbed "Black Coster" in reference to his character.</p>
<p>A proclaimed devout warrior of Gethric and defender of the faithful, the Count of Melmbirth has managed to arrange an impressive array of allies beyond Garmeley to support him in his cause, well needed considering the military dominance of the southerners during the war so far. Whether or not rumours of him acting as the puppoet for foreign interests that would like to see rich Garmeley destroyed, torn apart, brought to its kneels or simply purged of heresy are true is impossible to say, certain is however that Black Coster has invited many vipers into his nest that shall all want a piece of the pie that is Garmeley. The Count is married to Princess Lena of <a href="Eldmar" class="internal-link">Eldmar</a> , the youngest daughter of Queen Moira. </p>
