<p>Nestor Liston was born in the north Urien town of Gardhil in 837PA and joined the local clergy on his 12th birthday. He graduated his Minor Cudra at the University of Uhl in 854PA and completed his pilgrimage to Iztaw soon after, upon which he began his clerical studies in the Holy City itself. Liston became a Minister of the Church at the age of 28, finishing his Maior Cudra and Tregesia in the year 865PA.</p>

<p>In the year 899PA, Nestor was called upon to become the Bishop of St. Fredrics Cathedral in Niedrest, one of the most important Bishoprics in Urland and has served in this role ever since. He is a well-beloved member of the local community and has many friends within the church, owing to his exploits as a wandering cleric before his calling to the bishopric and his active refusal to take part in the political power games of Iztaw itself. As a lifelong student of the holy teachings, Nestor is said to command considerable powers and channel miracles through his hands in the images of the Saints themselves. Nestors health over the years has diminished with many being surprised to see him still alive and preparing for his 90th birthday in the even of this year.</p>

<p>As Bishop of Niedrest he is under the authority of the Archbishop of Ur, Martin III and the High-Bisop of Iztaw, Orthel II.</p>
