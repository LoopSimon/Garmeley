<p>Lorana, last name unknown is the wisdom of the Shrine of Dimrod, located in the Mesovo Hills of Hainach, and has guarded the shrine and its surrounding village communities for over half a century. She is not only responsible for the shrine itself but also the observation of Dimrods Rites and the preservation of older reani religious traditions, but often more importantly acts as the de facto unifying voice and figure of leadership in times of crisis for the dozens of small villages in the eastern Reani mountains and hills.</p>
