<p>Miasa was the mythical Queen of the Hera, a tribe of humans in eastern Sirenna, that is said to have brought harmony to Land and People and ascended as an Avatar of the Goddess Darmiga. Aspects of her are worshipped as a deity within the greater Darmiga Faith and many rulers and heroes of the Hera claim to be descendants of her to this day. </p>
