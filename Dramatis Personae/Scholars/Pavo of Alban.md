<p>A Meadi Wizard and prior to taking his leave the youngest Rector in the history of <a href="Korsfall College" class="internal-link">Korsfall College</a>, he is known as one of the greatest Magical Authorities alive among non-elven Practitioners and currently resides in he Tower of Alban in southern Meadrath. Aside from his prolific academic career and various exploits in politics and the like, von Alban is known for his extravagant dresses and style of fashion.</p>
<p>Little is known about his early life and childhood prior to his studies, he grew up in Pelling near Wormill a smaller town at the Trinmo river and was discovered by one of the Ichorhand Agents of Korsfall at the age of 7 that had travelled through the town, soon he would be taken to the College as is practice and receive an outstanding education in mathematics, astronomy and most importantly arcane studies. He graduated as a full candidate at the age of 17 in the winter of 907 and went on to become the personal aide and student of the legendary Muriel von Gloss. After 10 years under her guidance Pavo was recommended and controversially elected as the to this date youngest ever Rector of the College at only 27 years of age and granted the privilege of ichor licta as is tradition. His stay at the College ended in the later summer of 922 and Pavo left Korsfall College behind to construct his own Tower at Alban, granted to him by the Count of Merrel under unknown circumstances.</p>
<p>Today the Wizard is the subject of many conspiratorial theories and mysteries, especially after during the early months of 923 one of his two students, a scholar by the name of Cyril Effrin, vanished into thin air including the smaller tower he resided within that had been built adjacent to Pavos own domicile as well as multiple private students.</p>
