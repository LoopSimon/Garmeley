<p>Oleksandr IV was the last Monarch of the Kingdom of Halgrach up until his death in 834PA, after which the countries monarchy was dissolved and it was restructured under the Ushkyn System of the Republic of Grachy. He ruled Halgrach for 23 years from 811PA and ascended the Throne at the age of 26, following the death of his father, King Fyodor VII. Oleksandr was a military leader and famously fought in the <a href="Brothers War" class="internal-link">Brothers War</a> against the neighbouring country of Vihr, leading his countries forces to many victories against the numerically superior and much larger country. He died following a minor engagement at the Battle of Gorling Vale in the Fall of 834PA. </p>
