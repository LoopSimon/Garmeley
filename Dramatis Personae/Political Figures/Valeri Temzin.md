<p>Valeri Temzin was a dwarven member of the Kherovan Inquisition that acted as the Ambassador for the Khatrym and representative of the Open Face Tribunal for southern Urland during the late 9th and early 10th century. He fell victim to an unknown assailant during the events of the <a href="Niedrest Incident" class="internal-link">Incident</a> of 923PA.</p>
<p>Temzins death at the hands of an unknown assailant was no isolated incident as around the same time the entirety of the kherovan delegation  to Niedrest, and with that southern Urland was wiped out. The diplomatic fallout of this has been slow but steady, kherovan demands for increased powers and protection within foreign countries have either been begrudgingly accepted or so vehemently denied that as reaction various western administrations have full on withdrawn from agreements made during the Treaty of Kulum. </p>
