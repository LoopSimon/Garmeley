<p>Zazir Ibn Brahmis, sometimes just called "Brahmis", is the current Emir of Tusul and the personification of Elbae tyranny and rule over the Zipivi. He was infamously responsible for encouraging and then brutally putting down the Uprising of 925PA and has been described as ruthless, cunning and utterly loyal to the Sultanat. </p>
