<p>Ivanova Orin is the current Gram of the northern Grachwoods, encompassing the towns of Wangrad and Kharnov, the Ruins of the former roymer capital of Royba, and large stretches of the northern Grachwoods. Her family owns the Whytesmoke Distilleries that produces various liquors including the extremely popular Whytesmoke Vodka. She is a known supporter of Bagram Zemov on the greater grachian political stage but has otherwise preferred a softer touch in terms of her rule, favouring personal financial success over strong governance. With the Grachwoods being essentially no mans land she has done little to limit the various Boyars within the Region in their extensive abuse of the people and countryside.</p>
<p>The old fortress at Wangrad had long been the central hub for military authority within the region, but with the younger and prospering town of Kharnov rising up from the remnants of a former Roymer settlement Ivanova has started to move most of her fortune to the western town.</p>
