<p>Vormil III, Ras zi Vihr, Shield of the Faith and Lord-Protector of Wermia has ruled the mighty Rascy zi Vihr for almost 26 years, following his ascension to power in 901PA. He succeeded his grandfather, Ras Jakub I also known as "Good Jakub" due to the early death of his father, the Kisras Miskomir zi Ralpia. Vormil is the 12th Ras of the zi Ralpia Dynasty and regarded as the fourth of the "Farmer Lords", a term describing him and the three previous rulers of Vihr, Ras Jerviz I, Orian III and Jakub I, noting their exceptionally peaceful rule compared to the bloody decades preceding them. Vormil enjoys great popularity among the commonfolk and is known for the many roads built under his rule.</p>
<p>The Ras has been married to  Gras Maroza zi Wogrov, of the ruling House of <a href="Rovgi" class="internal-link"> Vadcy of Rovgi</a>, and the pair has four surviving children: Kisras Vasczo zi Ralpia, Kisa Yelizaveta zi Ralpia, Kis <a href="Jakub Ralpia" class="internal-link">Jakub zi Ralpia</a> and Kisa Anissa zi Ralpia. </p>
<p>Vormil has chosen to wear the Sapphire Band of <a href="Rulers of Vihr" class="internal-link"> Kasimir I</a>, a somewhat controversial decision but many point to similarities between the two monarchs in retrospect, given their strengths and struggles. His relationship with the current High-Bisop of <a href="Church of Iztaw" class="internal-link">Iztaw</a> can be described as amicable, although Vormil has taken a traditionalist stance on many theological debates that have arisen during his rule, such as the role of Women in the clergy and the scriptorium of the White Book.</p>
<p>Vormils estranged relationship with his two sons, his heir Vasczo and his younger son Jakub, has been the black spot on his otherwise largely white vest of governance, although the nature of conflict is very different between the two: Whereas Vasczos differences are largely in regards to political and academic reform and a restriction of the Clergys role within governance, Jakubs "controversies" and his eager committal of the <a href="Knights of the Unicorn" class="internal-link">Knights of the Unicorn</a> into the <a href="Garmian Succession Crisis" class="internal-link">Garmish Civil War</a> against the prior expressed wishes of his father have caused a wider and more public rift between father and son.</p>
