<p>Ivanova Orin is the current Gram of the northern Grachwoods, encompassing the towns of Wangrad and Kharnov, the Ruins of the former roymer capital of Royba, and large stretches of the northern Grachwoods. Her family owns the Whytesmoke Distilleries that produces various liquors including the extremely popular Whytesmoke Vodka. She is a known supporter of Bagram <a href="Ilja Zemov" class="internal-link"> Zemov</a> on the greater grachi political stage but has otherwise preferred a softer touch in terms of her rule, favouring personal financial success over strong governance. With the Grachwoods being essentially no mans land she has done little to limit the various Boyars within the Region in their extensive abuse of the people and countryside.</p>
<p>The old fortress at Wangrad had long been the central hub for military authority within the region, but with the younger and prospering town of Kharnov rising up from the remnants of a former Roymer settlement, named Breel, Ivanova has started to move most of her fortune to the western town. While she resides within the Fortress at Wangrad, her eldest son and heir Limsky has been put in charge of the Whytesmoke Distilleries at Kharnov and is the current Head of the City Ushkyn.</p>
