<p>Tadeuz Pilsud is the current Sekretar of Vihr, one of the chief advisors to his majesty King Vormil III and the Head of the Vaergriff, the Vihri Intelligence & Security Service. A physically unimpressive man little is known about Tadeuz past and although rumours about him coming from lesser nobility have existed since he entered the political stage it is an open secret he is a commoner. An exceptionally clever man and having studied at Szivek University he is one of the most influential men of the Realm. His loyalty lies with the Crown and the Crown only, having frequently come in conflict with the various Vadcy of the country and being feared by many of them. He holds an honorary Seat in the Honorary Assembly as the only member not of noble blood and acts as the representative of the Throne within the Institution.</p>
