<p>Prince Miriam Ispe Pikris, second of her Name, is the current Ruler of Hainach and a direct descendant of Prince Theris I.<br>
Following the death of her father, the late Prince Lantimor I, the Princedom of Hainach is now in the Hands of his oldest daughter of fourteen years, with her younger brother Lantimor as current heir-apparent. Most affairs of Governance are being handled by a Regent Council until the coming of Age of Miriam, which is under the leadership of Prince Yorgos of Diniv, the Princes uncle by marriage.</p>
<p>Prince Miriams is currently unbetrothed, a subject of much dissonance within the Princedom as many of the powerful nobles within Hainach see her youth and the current precarious political situation considering Hainachs involvement in the  <a href="Garmish Succession Crisis" class="internal-link">Garmish War</a> as an opportune moment to consolidate power for themselves.</p>
