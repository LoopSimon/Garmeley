<p>Ilya Zemov, the Current Bagram of the Holy Ushkyn Republic of Grachy has held the highest office within the country for fourteen years. He was elected by the Grand Ushkyn in 913PA and re-elected after his first term in 922PA as the head of the Federalist Faction. Among his most loyal supporters is the Lord-General of the Republic, <a href="Vasily Surovsky" class="internal-link">Vasily Surovsky</a> and the two are said to be childhood friends. Aside from the Capital region his powerbase lies in Run, where he ruled as Gram before his ascension and which is now ruled by his younger brother, Gram Oleg Zemov.</p>
<p>Zemovs internal politics have been in favour of native assimilation and forest settlements while as the head of the Federalists he represents the ideal of a decentralized Republic with only military matters decidedly in the hands of the Grand Ushkyn but large economic and legal freedoms for the individual Gram of the Republic. He has welcomed foreign trade and excessively expanded the nostroyan mining industries in the Grachhyll, causing several instances of armed conflicts and skirmishes with the free tribes of the mountains.</p>
<p>His foreign policy has favoured <a href="Vihr" class="internal-link">Vihr</a> while maintaining a strong and aggressive stance against <a href="Ukiolla" class="internal-link">Ukiolla</a>, actively continuing the Great Eastern War, he is said to be on amicable terms with the current Ras of the Realm of the Horn, <a href="Vormil III" class="internal-link">Vormil III</a>. The Bagram is a devout <a href="Gethricism" class="internal-link">gethric</a> and follower of <a href="Igor" class="internal-link">Saint Igor</a>, but has been outspoken about restricting the political power the Clergy and the Archbishopric of Nostroya should place within grachy politics. His personal wealth is rumoured to have enjoyed a large increase over his stay as Bagram and he has weathered considerably political opposition and active civil unrest, such as the infamous Derianov uprising in 919PA.</p>
