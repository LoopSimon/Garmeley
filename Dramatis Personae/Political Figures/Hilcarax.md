<p>Hilcarax the Misteater is a massive Mirani Safir of red skin and complexion. He resides within the Temple of the Singer in the City of Koshtir, ruling it as its High Priest. He is infamous for becoming a Yarimanji by killing and absorbing the Yari of the Mists, Pnurfezec. This has granted him many supernatural abilities beyond his already deep spiritual connection to the world, supposedly giving him the powers of prescience. He shows no signs of aging anymore, presumably a bi-product of his Yarimanji nature, but has maintained the massive shape and size of an elderly Mirani. Hilcarax efforts are directed to further the quest of the Listeners and Koshtir has become a devout and zealous community in service to the Singer under his rule. Though not technically mortal, he has ruled the City as its High Priest for almost 300 years, making him one of the if not the single longest reigning Leader currently alive.</p>
