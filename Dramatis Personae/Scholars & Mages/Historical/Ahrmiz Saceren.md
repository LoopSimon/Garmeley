<p>Better known as the infamous Necromancer Ahrmiz the Conjurer, this <a href="Kherova" class="internal-link">kheri</a> Wizard ruled over the now ruined city of Kulum in the 9th century, although he was killed by brave adventurers his Cult has spread wide across Ilrakhan and rumours are well alive that he may attempt to reclaim life on the material Plane again. Following his defeat, the kherovan <a href="Kherovan Inquisition" class="internal-link">Inquisition</a> ruled the city lost to the lingering curses and the Kherovan Government sponsored the settlement of New Kulum which grew rapidly in the last century.</p>
