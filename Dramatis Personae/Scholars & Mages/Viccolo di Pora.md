<p>Perhaps one of the most famous and infamous practitioners of the arcane alive in our times, Archmage di Pora is the face and leading voice of the di-Pora Revolution; the academic movement against clerical oppression of magical research and practice through the Wermian Accords. He is most famous for his public renouncement of the entire Gethric clergy and faith in 856PA and currently lives under the protection of the Prince of Battvia in Ciravelli Tower.</p>
<p>Di Poras parentage is unknown but his status as a half-elf has always been attached to him for better or worse. Within perhaps the most liberal region in Ilrakhan, Cevolis, bigotry against half-elves is largely directed at Rogyn born elfs and the militant struggles of nations such as Turpador with the Woodland Realm, not so much with beheran-born individuals. His racial considerations have only added to his mythos as a scholarly revolutionary of diversity and freedom of thought, as well as underlined his strong anti-gethric stance, considering the original Gethric Schism that birthed Trinicism occurred as a response to elven pogroms during the 8th century.</p>
<p>Under tutelage by the former archmage of Ciravelli, Leonardo Manracelli, he is now and has been in the past, deeply ingrained in Cevoli politics, but notable only took on a supportive and peacemaking role during the numerous battvian and paper-city wars of recent memory, likely aware of his utter reliance on both nations support of his cause in lieu of international ire.</p>
