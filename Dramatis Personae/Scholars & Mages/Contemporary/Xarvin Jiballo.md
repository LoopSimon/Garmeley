<p>Professor Xarvin Jiballo is an alumni of the Institute for Practical Energies of Almadus and is known as one of the leading voices for modern automative Aritficery of our time. His work is largely based on that of his teacher, the legendary Gnome Charatov Podlim and concerns mostly minor more efficient single-core Automata, such as his famous invention the Thopter. Jiballo is an avid enjoyer of recreational archeology and has been accused of stealing religious and cultural relics by critics, a notion he vehemently rejects. </p>
<p>Xarvin Jiballo was born to a elven mother and human father in the port city of Almadin, within the southern <a href="Iz-Almadus" class="internal-link">Emirate</a>. His father is a high-ranking member of the local government and his family one of the richest within the city, sponsoring Xarvins scientific career.</p>
<p>The Professor, although based in southern Almadus, has been known to travel exceedingly far away from his homeland, even partaking in a diplomatic Mission to the yppori Island Nation of <a href="Hanshi" class="internal-link">Hanshi</a> during which he assisted an elbae delegation with his linguistic prowess. Jiballo reportedly supports many elements of the academic group of the <a href="Luminists" class="internal-link">Luminists</a>, although due to their nature as a mainly Ilrakhani circle and his clear associations with the beheran sphere and sultanate programs, the sentiment is often seen as largely performative at best. </p>
