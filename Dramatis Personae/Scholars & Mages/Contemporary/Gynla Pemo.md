<p>Gynla Pemo is a renowned beheran Alchemist that has worked notably independent of the <a href="The Alchemists Guild" class="internal-link">Guild</a> and was long respected as one of the most innovative and ground-breaking voices in academic discourse. His unorthodox methods and fields of research gnawed away at his credibility, success and appeal over the decades until he was openly shunned by many. Pemos current whereabouts are a mystery.</p>
