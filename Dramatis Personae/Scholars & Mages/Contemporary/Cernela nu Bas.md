<p>Cernela nu Bas is the Court Magus at Afgillion serving the ruler of Cimfon, currently Mael Ednyr III Prawyl. Her role is primarily that of an advisor to the Mael on all things mystical and magical as well as dealing with threats of supernatural nature, but has always encompassed important diplomatic duties with foreign realms and institutions, specifically academic organizations. As Cimfon isn't, and never has been part of the <a href="Orenburg Pact" class="internal-link">Orenburg Pact</a> her duties have included military applications of the arcane and countermeasures to opposing forces of similar talents.</p>
<p>Nu Bas has developed a reputation for theatrical displays of great power, reports of her being seen flying through their air, summoning large columns of flames and lightning itself are all overshadowed by her self-admitted love for transforming and manipulating the substance of Ink, creating shapes and forms, shields and arrows and manipulating the substance as if it was clay strung up like a marionette. It is this display that has earned her the nickname "Cernela of the Ink", a moniker she fully embraces with her appearance and public displays giving her a legendary state of fame in the Lands of Othirdon and beyond.</p>
