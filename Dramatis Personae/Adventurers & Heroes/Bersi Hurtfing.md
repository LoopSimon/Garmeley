<p>Bersi Hurtfing is a jueli member of the Mørbrig Chapter, having acted as an Envoy within Nostroya for the better part of the last decade, he joined the Chapter as a child of 8 years old and has predominantly worked under the High Weavers of Juelrup. His talents fall in the realm of arcane history and observation and his role has predominantly been that of a diplomat and information gatherer. He is the bearer of the Rod of Nur and one of the most respective members of the Chapter, although he has never attempted to rise within the prestigious organization due to a lack of magical talent himself. </p>
<p>Hurtfing tends to travel alone without putting too much attention to himself, but has been repeatedly criticized by the higher Authorities within the Chapter for his tendencies to get side tracked and get caught up with local disputes and problems.</p>
