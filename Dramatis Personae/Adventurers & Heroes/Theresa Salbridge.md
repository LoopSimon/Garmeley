<p>Theresa Salbridge is the young and moderately successful owner and sole employee of Salbridge Investigations, an independent detective Agency founded in 822 in Grevec, Garmeley. Following the outbreak of the Garmian Civil War in 923, she aptly relocated further south to protect her business, clients would surely come either way. Theresas trusty pony and companion is named Hakon.</p>
<p>In May of the year 923PA she would contract a group of adventurers in the reani village of Kontini, seeking help to find a lost potential client named Oroyo she had agreed to meet. Together with four mysterious travellers she would venture into the eastern Reani Hills and find herself confronted with a conflict between the Laughing Cloud Mercenaries and a Native Tribe of Dimrod Worshippers.</p>
