<p>The Sword of the Sea, is a living legend in the Rean region, having bested foul beasts, ended vile sorcerers, revealed rogue-ish plots and put down too many robber outfits to count. Bivaldi was raised in the traditions of the <a href="Knights of the Spring" class="internal-link">Knights of the Spring</a>, by an unknown mentor and is often considered to be the last of their order. His first known exploit was the duel at Tirbrige, where an only 17 year old Bivaldi is said to have valiantly put down a devious Troll and rid the lands of his influence and terror; this would be noly the first notch in a belt of heroics and legends surrounding the Knight.</p>
<p>Bivaldi was part of the group that took down the Red Ahool of the Vale, exposed Baron Midil during the Grevenni Crisis of 901 and ended the live of Jonek Two-Tongues after he had killed the Duchess of Garmeley. Notably Bivaldi had essentially retired afterwards blaming himself for her death, friends often reported him regretting the choice to pursue Jonek instead of saving the Duchess, considering the eventual bloody developments of the <a href="Garmish Succession Crisis" class="internal-link">Garmish Civil War</a>.</p>
<p>Mikael still travels the Lands of the Stormy Sea to visit old friends but avoids the lands touched by the war, he spends more and more time in Reanport where his two children reside and have started a printing business.</p>
