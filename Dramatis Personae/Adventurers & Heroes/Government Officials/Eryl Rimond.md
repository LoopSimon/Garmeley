<p>Sir Eryl Rimond of the Olvale is the current Captain of the Gelreas Border Station and Point Meso Bridge, overviewing the incoming and outgoing traffic between the Sultanate and the Princedom across the Mesovo River. He served in the Princes Guard between 907 and 917PA and was promoted to the important position of Captain of the Bridge as a reward for outstanding service. Fiercely loyal and a patriot, Rimonds main quality is his cool headed approach to potentially explosive situations, careful but decisive hand for political issues and good report with the elven military across the Border. Him and Bey <a href="Neoda Isheb" class="internal-link">Isheb</a> have worked together for over a decade now and consider each other friends.</p>
