<p>Razzan is a seasoned beheran warrior and the current Captain of the Laughing Cloud Mercenary Band, he stands at an unnaturally tall height of almost seven feat and wields both a Scimitar and a gnomish prototype rifle, a rare sight in these parts. He and his band of over a dozen warriors are employed by the Vasser Cartel to safe its interests in northern Behera.</p>
<p>Hector is the 3rd Leader of the Band: following its eponymous founder Irguan ibn-Kor, the 2nd Captain of the Laughing Cloud adopted a young Hector who grew up in the tight-knit formation, to Razzan the group is his family and legacy both.</p>
<p>During May of the year 927PA, Hector would be confronted with a difficult situation when, while escorting the eccentric Professor <a href="Xarvin Jiballo" class="internal-link">Jiballo</a>, both his client and three of his warriors were taken hostage by local reani Dimrod-Worshippers.</p>
