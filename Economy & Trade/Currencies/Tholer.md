<p>A popular currency used in the regions of Geol, Dandry and the Runsby Islands, first introduced by geoli traders and quickly adopted by the northern Kingdom of Spursley as well. Notably the Bilsworth Ministry no longer has much use for the currency anymore and only rarely uses it as a way to exchange services with outsiders, relying on a different form of exchange internal due to their citizens unique circumstance.</p>
