<p>The Gram, sometimes known as glister, is a catch all term for the various evaluations and minting principles of Gram in the dwarvish Holds and Gnomish Kingdoms of the Mountains in Inara. Due to individual contracts certain denominations of the Gram, such as the Ottian and Xer-koli Gram have set exchange rates with the Gryphon and are often used interchangeably.</p>
