<p>The Grim, sometimes known as glister, is a catch all term for the various evaluations and minting principles of Grim in the dwarvish Holds and Gnomish Kingdoms of the Mountains in Inara. Due to individual contracts certain denominations of the Grim, such as the Ottian and Xer-koli Grim have set exchange rates with the Gryphon and are often used interchangeably.</p>
