<p>The currency, or rather a collection of currencies used by the Empire of Pravazik and various Safiri realms of central and southern Behera, based on the blood trade of the Yari controlled realms like Yokosch and Pravazik its one of the least reliable and softest currencies and nations such as Koshtir have long abandoned its use.</p>
