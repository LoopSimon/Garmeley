<p>The Gryphon of Uhl, sometimes known as the urien Gryphon, is the most popular currency on the continent of Ilrakhan, it was widely adopted following the War of Horn & Fang and confirmed as the national currency of the two global powers of Vihr and Meadrath. Its printing is regulated within the Treaty of Uhl and largely handled by the Mint of Uhl. Prior to the War the Gryphon was already widespread in Trykkerog and Urland.</p>
