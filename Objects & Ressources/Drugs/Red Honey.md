<p>This recreational and highly addictive drug is traded around Hainach, the Sultanate, Grevenna and even as far east as Azuvke and the Hierarchy.</p>
<p>It is an opioid synthesized from an unknown source, first gaining notoriety in the 920s for its destructive habits, high fatality and rich mark up. Red honey is typically diluted in water or alcohol and consumed orally as a drink, but it can also be cooked and inhaled. </p><p>Its effects include euphoria, hallucinations, concentration, reduced fatigue and increased libido although reports are unreliable and no large scale studies have been conducted.</p>
