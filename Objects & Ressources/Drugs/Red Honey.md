<p>This recreational and highly addictive drug is traded around Hainach, the Sultanate, Grevenna and even as far east as Azuvke and the Hierarchy.</p>
<p>It is an opioid synthesized from an unknown source, first gaining notoriety in the 920s for its destructive habits, high fatality and rich mark up. Red honey is typically diluted in water or alcohol and consumed orally as a drink, but it can also be cooked and inhaled. Its effects include euphoria, hallucinations, concentration, reduced fatigue and increased libido although reports are unreliable and no large scale studies have been conducted.</p>

<p>A new variant of the Drug, often associated with it but never proven to be chemically even related, known as "Demons Blood", "Red Blood" or "Honey Blood" has appeared in the Asef and the Pilvian Gulf in the fall of 926PA. It is said to act similar to Red Honey but also are said to further include acting as a steroid for magical capabilities, as well as induce mutation and physical deformity as well as insanity in specific subjects.</p>
