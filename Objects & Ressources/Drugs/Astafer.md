<p>A drug created from the dried latex of Asta Poppies, Astafer is a synthetic opioid that is extremely common within the Sultanate, its various substates and the Lands of Kaito. Asta-poppies only grow on the Fitta Islands in the Purple Sea, and the trade income of the drug is a substantial reason for the regional dominance of the Hanshi Realm.</p>

<p>The effects of Astafer act psycho-activating and are often associated with hallucinations, increased awareness, spiritual experiences and pain relief. It is notably used within the Sultanates higher circles to induce trances and has been seen in experimental military environments as well.</p>
