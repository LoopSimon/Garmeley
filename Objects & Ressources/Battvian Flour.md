<p>Battvian Flour, sometimes referred to as "blue Flour" is a stimulant drug produced from the leaves of the Zegano plant, that can only be found in Palmunia, Rakh and Cyraclion and is therefore extremely rare. Almost all Flour production in the world takes place in the Paper Cities and Battvia, but the drug is traded in Urland, Meadrath and all of Cevolis actively, with many cartels employing dwarven routes.</p>

<p>Commonly smoked or snorted, Battvia Flour is a fast acting drug upon use, its effects including euphoria, hyper-focus, cardiovascular hypertension, hyperthermia, restlessness and increased spiritual awareness.</p>
