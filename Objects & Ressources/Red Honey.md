<p>This recreational and highly addictive drug is traded around Hainach, the Sultanate, Grevenna and even as far east as Azuvke and the Hierarchy.</p>
<p>It is an opioid synthesized from an unknown source, first gaining notoriety in the 920s for its destructive habits, high fatality and rich mark up. Red honey is typically injected, usually into a vein, but it can also be inhaled. </p><p>Its effects include euphoria, hallucinations, concentration, reduced fatigue and increased libido although reports are unreliable and no large scale studies have been conducted.</p>
