<p>The Orenburg Pact is an understanding to prohibit members of official circles or institutions of higher magical learning and academia to actively participate in armed conflicts such as wars, following the devastating use of magic during the <a href="War of Horn and Fang" class="internal-link">War of Horn and Fang</a>. Notable it forbids its members from contributing in destructive manners on the field of battle, not the relieve of the wounded or sick or other more behind the scenes operations.
It is often mentioned in the same breath as the <a href="Treaty of Uhl" class="internal-link">Treaty of Uhl</a> however is at best signed by government sanctioned institutions rather than actual heads of states and a more academic arrangement.</p>
<p>As almost all institutions of higher learnings require documents and identification upon enrolling and graduating within higher  classes of learning and, among the larger ones such as Korsfall, employ extra methods of control over their graduates enforcement has been very successful and no major war in central Ilrakhan has seen a breach of the Orenburg Pact yet.</p>
<p>The agreement threatens punishments ranging mutilation and banishment at the low end to death at the higher and for breach of the Pact, due to it concerning major political events it is usually thought of as easy to keep track of, although rumours about behind the scenes actions and veiled special operations during armed conflicts have started essentially immediately after the creation of the Pact.</p>
