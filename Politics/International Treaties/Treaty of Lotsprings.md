<p>Signed on the 16th of May 900PA, the Treaty of Lotsprings regulates customs and trade between Blijgrat and the Realms of the Meadi Crown, trade relations of the G.U.V. and G.W.H. on the continent and in Sertova & Othirdon, unified charters in international ports for ships of both companies and the colonization of lands west of the Tirean Ocean. Furthermore the treaty essentially guarantees mutual military defence of Company holdings, within international waters, on foreign shores and reaffirms the Continental Borders. </p>
<p>The growing rivalry following the so called "Opening of the West" in the 800s of Meadi and Weadi traders and sailors, as well as common interests and mutual enemies in Sertovi, Othiri, Cevoli and Trykkerogi commercial Unions and traders brought the two dominant entities of the Meadi Empire, encompassing the Kingdom itself as well as all its vassals, and the Blijgrat League, including the incredibly industrious and successful Geldfield Unie van Vrijhandel. The meadi Gesellschaft für Westmeadischen Handel was created as a somewhat mirroring entity and unified Company to the G.U.V and for lack of better words, the Ports and Lands of Othirdon, Sertova and all of Aisligos were essentially divided between the two. This alliance not only guaranteed a dominant military union in the wake of potential conflicts for both countries, most importantly the weaker League still worrying about northern invasions from the past, but also an incredibly tight grip on all western Trade that would inform most international policy for both countries in the centuries to come. </p>
