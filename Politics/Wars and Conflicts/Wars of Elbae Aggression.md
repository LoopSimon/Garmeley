Series of Conflicts between the [[Elbae]] Sultanate and the Reani Coalition

750PA - 815PA

### **First War of Elbae Aggression**
*750PA-762PA*

Sultanate: Elbae Sultanate, Emirate of [[Iz-Almadus]]
Reani Coalition: Lordship of  [[Grevenna]], Khatrym of  [[Kherova]], Princedom of [[Hainach]], Kingdom of [[Vihr]]

Result: Elbae retreat behind the Horad.


### **Second War of Elbae Aggression**
*781PA-790PA*

Sultanate: Elbae Sultante, Emirate of Iz-Almadus
Reani Coalition: Lordship of Grevenna, Princedom of Hainach, Kingdom of Vihr, Duchy of Garmeley, Khatrym of Kherova

Result: Subjugation of Hainach.



### **Third War of Elbae Aggression**
*805PA-815PA*

Sultanate: Elbae Sultante, Emirate of Iz-Almadus, Princedom of Hainach
Reani Coalition: Lordship of Grevenna, Kingdom of Vihr, Duchy of Garmeley

Result: [[Treaty of Danipol]].
