Belligerents: The Kingdoms of  [[Halgrach]] and [[Vihr]].

*Date:  822 PA - 835 PA 


*Location:* Vihr, Grachy, Torn Sea

*Result:* White Peace, End of the Kingdom of Halgrach

<p>At the end of the Borthers War, the King of Halgrach died on the battlefield while commanding a weak position against the advise of his general staff and the supporting grachy nobility. As sentiment had turned Oleskandr, who was seen as having squandered an unlikely victory against the numerically superior foe of Vihr,  his death was a turning point in the History of the grachy who organised the country under the Ushkyn System into the Grachy Republic.
The Grachy Generals sued for peace with vihr who had been at the backfoot for a majority of the war as the internal change in Grachy demanded a peace as fast as possible.</p>


<p>Vihr never forgot the shameful result of the war in their eyes and even among the more content and otherwise oriented voices, Vormil II is remembered negatively. His epitaph as the "Axebreaker" begin self given following the war due to the dissolution of Halgrach, whos symbol had always been the Axe,  taking on an almost mocking aspect essentially immediately after the peace. </p>
