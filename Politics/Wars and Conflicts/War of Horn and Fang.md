Belligerents:  The Kingdoms of  [[Vihr]] and [[Meadrath]] , including several of their respective dependencies.

*Date: 851 PA - 876 PA


*Location:* Northern Urland, Torn Sea

*Result:* [[Treaty of Uhl]], [[Orenburg Pact]]

While already representing one of the longest and bloodiest Wars between some of the known worlds largest nations, this war was particularly notable for its use of the significant amount of magical powers employed by both sides, largely to negative reactions of magical institutions on both sides, eventually leading to the famous [[Orenburg Pact]].
