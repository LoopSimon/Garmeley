<p>The Grand Duchy of Martella is a country in southern Sertovis, bounded by the Lions Bay to its west and the Smoking Sea to its South it shares borders with <a href="Revelle" class="internal-link">Revelle</a> and <a href="Erlmondy" class="internal-link">Erlmondy</a> on the mainland. As a former part of the greater Kingdom of <a href="Cevolis" class="internal-link">Cevolis</a>, it is, although now fully independent and largely removed from cevolian political struggles for regional power, a country of dissonance and harmony both, the cultural exchange between cevoli and sertovi can be seen nowhere as well as within the borders of Martella and it has become a regional power on the peninsular with its own defined cultural identity.</p>
