<p> Situated alongside the former wermian border, Torry is one of the major Vadcy within Vihr. While geographically the smallest Vadcy of the Realm it holds a sizeable portion of the Vihri population in its coastal settlements. Its creation during the Council of 860 was cause for much uproar among the countries elite as it was seen as a clear move again encroaching church power.</p>
