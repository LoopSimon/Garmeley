<p>Levazci is one of the major Vadcy within Vihr, situated on the southern slopes of the Grachhyll alongside the Lehva River, its name literally translating to "Land of the Lehva". It holds significance as the border region with Grachy, as all wares and trade that leaves or enters Nostroya inlands is transported over the Hyllpass between Tuskwatch and Orzebachi.</p>
<h2>Geography</h2>
<p>Geographically, Levazci is limited by the Grachyll and Mistspire mountains to the east and defined by the Lehva River that exits north towards Szievek before flowing into Trazlov. The Region is mostly hilly, with mountains in the east and plain areas in the east. The climate alongside the Lehva is transitional temperate continental, overall the main influence of the climate is dry continental, with frequent cold north winds in winter.</p>
<p>Mammals such as goats, boars, wolves, foxes, wildcats and hares are characteristic for Levazci, but the Vadcy is most famous for its local blak quail.</p>
