<p>The Island of Droil was first settled over a thousand years ago by Locni from the mainland and small village communities would eventually grow into larger towns and relatively populous cities. Tragically the history of the people of Droil who would eventually identify themselves as their own culture, the droili not the locni, would be one of hardship and suffering.</p>
<p>The young droili community of merchant settlements and growing towns was hit harder than any other area by the Wrath of Isara, the massive Earthquake and ensuing Tsunami that struck Othirdon and Sertova in the late 3rd century PA. The already harsh climate could only support limited settlements at this point in history, a fragile state of affairs that was devastatingly disrupted by the natural disaster.</p>
<p>Tintarol, first united as a tribal Monarchy in the 190s would later establish itself as a powerful Locni Kingdom during the centuries of emancipation when the Great Empires of <a href="Aerlan" class="internal-link">Aerlan</a> and <a href="Thirdonis" class="internal-link">Thirdonis</a> fell and new nations emerged. The war loving Maeli of <a href="Tintarol" class="internal-link">Tintarol</a> claimed ownership over the island of Droil in the 5th century with little to no resistance from a weakened Aerlan embroiled in civil war, that had previously called themselves the overlords of the island instead while neither nation truly enforced military power or levied substantial taxes on the small communities. </p>
<p>Through hardship, growing trade relationships with south and east and a reputation for a harsh but fair destination in the far west where everything was possible, the droili villages and communities eventually grew into towns and even cities. The people of the island embraced the ideas of Panrity as it was seen as a harmony between traditional Spirit Cults and the conservative Kalbinist Faith itself. </p>
<p>Unofrtunately, in 536PA yet another horror would befall the people of Droil as the powerful and terrifying Baeling Empire attacked Tintarol and Droil, conquering the northern parts of Tintarol, in the modern day owned by <a href="Meadrath" class="internal-link">Meadrath</a>, as well as looting and conquering Droil itself. Living under the Empire was essentially a great ressource drain on the Droili who were also submitted to religious dogma, forced to practice Gethricism under Baeling Warlords. While the <a href="Baeling Empire" class="internal-link">Baeling Empire</a> would eventually fall and northern Tintarol became part of Meadrath when Weadland and Zeeport were abandoned by the Empire as well, Droil was not to be so lucky as to be simply left alone. The ever resistant and fiercely traditional warlike Kingdom of Vignasoya would instead take over governance of the island and use it as a staging ground for raids, conquests and its many seafaring adventures across the great western Ocean that would eventually lead to the discovery of a new World in Aisligos. What were great heroics for Skori Sailors would be perhaps the worst period in the history of Droil as an island, its native population essentially enslaved and oppressed by the foreigners once again.</p>
<p>Eventually, with the help of Ravlander, Tintaroli and Cimfoni ships and warriors, the people of Droil would successfully stage a rebellion against their skori rulers in the year 772PA and free itself from Vignasoya although now once again under the much more organised and present rule of Tintarol, yet another price paid for small steps towards liberation.</p>
<p>Growing levies in lue of a failing Monarchy in Tintarol, the constant disregard or acknowledgement of droili cultural identity and the ever growing industrial and primarily mercantile importance of Droils ports during the early stages of Aisligos colonisation would lead to proper independence movements growing on the island in the mid 800s. The Ruler of Droils unofficial capital and largest city, the harbour town of Iskerburg, was a man by the name of Jesper Isk  that would end up heading the movement. In fear of direct confrontation with Droil as a unified entity in its current state and unwilling to risk losing its possession of the island, the Mael of Tintarol declared Droil an autonomous Herzad under the Mael of Tintarol and granted Jesper the title of first Herz of Droil in 863PA. While this was initially seen and celebrated publicly as a victory for the droili people, many have critiqued Jesper as a lackey who took an easy path to personal power and prestige instead of a hard path to full on independence from their ancient overlords. These voices have only grown louder as the island continues to become more and more valuable in the Age of cross-tirean colonisation and greater international trading networks, as trouble seems to be brewing once again in the Herzad of Droil.</p>
