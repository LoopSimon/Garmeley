<p>The Great Kingdom of Urland, named after the region of Urland between the Kortopi and Trollback mountains, was a feudal Kingdom that existed in the Silver and Steel Age. With strong chivalric and martial traditions it was at times the strongest political entity on the face of Ilrakhan but would succumb to a splintering of its Urien subcultures following the Fall of West Urland, when the Hobgoblins of <a href="Ak-Saray" class="internal-link">Ak-Saray</a> invaded at the end of the 7th century. Conflicts arose earlier when the old faith of the urien peoples, belief in the Turvik Gods, was slowly but surely supplanted by the emerging <a href="Gethricism" class="internal-link">Gethric Church.</a></p>
