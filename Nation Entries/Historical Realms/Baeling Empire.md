<p>This ephemeral bykmali ruled Empire was a thalassocracy, with many of its most important bases of power only connected by and dependent upon the Sea. At its heigh it ruled over almost all of Trykkerog, northern Urland, Weadland and many coastal cities and the Emperors of Baeling were among the most powerful rulers in all Ilrakhan. In 465PA, Valund Baeling, Lord of Alsebyn won the battle of Assafurd again the forces of King Sweyn of Grelm and declared himself the King of the Bay, his successors would take on his name and be forever known as the House of Baeling.</p>
<p>The Empire, as a maritime realm of military power, was constantly expanding its borders and would see its downfall arrive when the cracks in this permanent conquest began to show as later Baeling Emperors would fail to show control over new overseas conquests as seen with the liberation of Zeeport, gain new lands as seen during the failed Conquest of Halgrach and to control uprisings and opposition at home in Trykkerog, as seen during the constant uprising of the Skori Lords that would eventually full secede before the Empire collapsed. The strength of the early rulers, to project military might and constantly travel their realm would be the weakness of the late ones who could not keep up with the demands and were instead faced with a constant duty to fix the cracks in the failing realm.</p>
