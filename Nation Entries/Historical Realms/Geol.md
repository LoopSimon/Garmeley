<p>The Republic of Geol, often simply referred to as Ancient Geol, was a vahri realm that rose to power in the later centuries of the Age of Iron. Geol was founded around 400BA by vahri merchants and quickly grew into a dominant power among the local city states. The Shaftir, the Cities elected leader of war, at the time of the 280s was a man called Bahrbuhr whos unprecedented military victories against neighbouring cities granted him a position of secure power and wealth that ended up allowing him to declare himself King of Geol and all its surrounding lands. Bahrbur and his dynasty would conquer large sways of Vahr in the next century and establish Geol as a dominant power in Behera.</p>
<p>The Vahri peoples were, just like their northern cousins the Spurshey, a seafaring culture that settled and expanded into many of the island chains and archipelagos westwards, within the Sea of Clouds and the wider Tirean Ocean. Geoli expansion even stretched into the central Vinjiran Jungles and there are ancient records proving contact with Yppori and Pravi cities from as early as the 240s BA. Efforts were made to conquer the people of northern Dandry many a times although periods of long standing peace and exchange largely outweigh eras of war and conquest. Geol was in surprisingly eager exchange with <a href="Lorqis" class="internal-link">Lorqis</a>, the only remaining city of ancient <a href="Cyraclion" class="internal-link">Cyraclion</a> and much of what we know of early Spursey and Vahri history is due to records in the Great Library of Lorqis.</p>
<p>Geol fully embraced the teachings of Aelfred Dogger, the 2nd century Prophet and was previously worshipping a loosely related Pantheon of individual City Deities such as Geolzir, the patron deity of Geol itself.</p>
<p>In modern times almost all Vahri nations still proclaim the mantle of carrying on Geols legacy, primarily the realms of <a href="Landerly" class="internal-link">Landerly</a>, <a href="Nir Hadast" class="internal-link">Nir-Hadast</a> and <a href="Tarqin" class="internal-link">Tarqin.</a></p>
