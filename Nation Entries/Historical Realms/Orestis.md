<p>Named after the so called "oresti civilization" the League of Orestis describes a Union of powerful city states in central Cevolia of antiquity, their cultural influence has taken deep roots even almost 800 years after the end of the League with many elements of modern day cevoli culture being originally based of the oresti people and even massive bodies of water such as the Tirean Ocean being named after the Oresti Pantheon of Gods. The Capital and most important city within the League was Oris, which is why modern day scholars have decided on the name for the culture and federation although it is unclear what the word exactly meant. The League was in contact with the ancient <a href="Palmunia" class="internal-link">palmuni</a> and <a href="Rakh" class="internal-link">rakhi</a> cities and empires, as well as having a supposedly friendly and eager cultural exchange with the elfs of <a href="Rogyn" class="internal-link">Rogyn</a>.</p>
