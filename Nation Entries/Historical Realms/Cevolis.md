<p>The Kingdom of Cevolis was first declared as one of the earliest ilrakhani realms by the Lionkings of old who would go on to become the dominant force within the region we know as Cevolia in the modern day, following their rivalry with the ancient Realm of Orestis, but ended up being conquered and subjugated themselves by the Empire of <a href="Rakh" class="internal-link">Rakh</a> in the year 258BA. Following the demise of Rakh, the legendary Warlord Lingolio declared himself once again King of the green lands and re-unified Cevolis in 180PA.</p>
<p> During the Age of Silver Cevolis reached uncontested dominance over the Smoky Sea and invaded and settled large party of Sertova beyond the Forest of Rogyn, the Lions Legacy in the western peninsular is still felt today as cevoli influence remains strong in nations such as <a href="Martella" class="internal-link">Martella</a>. </p>
<p>The House of Molina that ruled over Cevolis during the 4th and 5th century ended in 509PA and none of the dominant nobles could project enough power across the large and already divided realm to maintain a coherent realm, beginning the age of the cevoli successor states.</p>
