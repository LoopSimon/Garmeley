<p>The Ancient Elven Dynasties refer generally to the four major Dynasties that ruled western Behera following Jirs unification of the Tomb Cities, originating from the reaches of the Za-Jira River and the southern coast of the Strait of Meter. The pinnacle of the Dynasties was achieved under the Meter dynasty, although one could argue that the re-establishment of elven rule following the eruption of Al-Akka in year 0PA and the resulting destruction of the Meter, under the Elbae can be seen as a direct succession to the Jir, Alparid, Rahaz and Meter of antiquity. During the almost 2000 years of rule, the elves established total dominance over the Hessef and all of western Behera, conquered the ancient human cities and kingdoms of the region and contested the Qalmyr and Yppori for maritime hegemony. Scientific discoveries, cultural innovations and bureaucratic institutions that have led to the modern Sultanates dominance over intercontinental space were almost all established during the varying periods of dynastic rule.</p>
