<p>Formerly the Dwarven Hold of Fogor-Kol, Fogorix fell into the Hands of Goblins in 626PA through mysterious means, likely related to the subsequent establishment of the <a href="Cult of the Darksun" class="internal-link">Cult of the Darksun</a> under Ogana. Tales of ghosts, moving shadows and nightmarish deaths are now mere myths, and the current diplomatic standing of Fogorix with its neighbours is surprisingly good, one could argue better than that of the reclusive dwarves of Fogor-Kol. The "corruption" of the <a href="Terem-Zal" class="internal-link">Terem-Zal</a> Axor-For and its transformation into the Darksun was largely the reason for the 2nd Great Underwar.</p>
