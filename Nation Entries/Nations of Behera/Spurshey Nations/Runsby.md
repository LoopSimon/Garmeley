<p>The Republic of Runsby, often referred to as Runland, is an island nation located on the Runsby & Lipley Isles, far off the Coast of western Behera. First a former colony of <a href="Spursley" class="internal-link">Kingdom of Spursley</a>, then a proper part of its Kingdom, the Isles have united under a single Banner ca a decade after the tragic fall of Spursley in 747PA. Unlike the <a href="Bilsworth" class="internal-link">Ministry of Bilsworth</a>, its citizens are not of the undead variety and both countries consider themselves the true successors of Spursley.</p>
