<p>The Tageyo, or just "Tageyo" is a location on the island of the same name , located in the southern Smaragd Sea of the cost of Kaito. It is the Apex point of the Tageyo-Field that supresses planar energy on the Beheran Continent.</p>
<p>The most common belief is that the origin of the Field lies within the history of the mysterious <a href="Yari" class="internal-link">Yari</a>, beings that belong both to the material and the spiritural Realm, the Myth of the "Yarigam" or "Yari Pact" speaks of the great gathering of these creatures on Tageyo thousands of years ago and is often attributed as the cause of the Field in folklore.</p>
<p>The Tageyo-Field covers an approximate radius of ca 2600km, but exact measurements are impossible to determine. What is known that its effect significantly increases the closer one finds themselves to the centre.</p>
