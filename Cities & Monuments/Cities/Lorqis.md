<p>Lorqis is the only continuously inhabited cyracli city from before the Great Fall, it is currently the largest walled city on Cyra. Lorqis was likely the major southern port of united Cyraclion for a majority of its time, although records of rivalries with a city called Hecvos exist. In current times Lorqis is almost exclusively inhabited by cyracli cyclopi of the old ways, and a near mythical destination for travellers and sailors seeking knowledge, secrets and legends of the past. The inhabitants of Lorqis are often considered by themselves and others to be only true remnants of ancient Cyraclion, although many descendants of the Usuni city states would claim differently, they are virtually unrelated culturally and within their societies cyclopi have become rare and are dwindling. The Great Library of Lorqis is often rumoured to be the Kiln of <a href="Alchemy" class="internal-link">kynotic</a> knowledge  and one of the greatest and oldest maintained sources of history on the entire globe, it has a near religious importance for many alchemists of our days.</p>
