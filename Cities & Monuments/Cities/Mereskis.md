<p>Mereskis is the third most populous city in the Elbae Sultanate, after <a href="Jiravid" class="internal-link">Jiravid</a> and Rahzalbis, and the second largest metropolis on the Stormy Sea after <a href="Reanport" class="internal-link">Reanport</a>. It sits on the southern Stormy Coast and is the capital of the Mereskis Province. </p>
<p>Mereskis has around 2500 years of recoded urban history, dating back to times before elven governance when the city was known as Samyr, even earlier dates for the beheran settlement that traded with pilvian city states to its north are recorded. Lying at the  mouth of a smaller Bay and on top of of a hill, Mereskis has been one of the principal mercantile cities of Ilrakhan for much of its history.</p>
<p>Mereskis, then known as Samyr, was the Capital of the Marival of Samyr that stretched out from the Mesovo to the coast of the Sunny Sea, before it was conquered and became part of the Kingdom of <a href="Rean" class="internal-link">Rean</a>, only to later on be conquered by the Sultans of Elbae and integrated into the elven Hegemony. Culturally it remains largely beheri as does most of the northern Sultante but is a true melting pot of racial and religious divergence, with Shirin of Nirydia, Bykmali of Trykkerog, Safir or sub Behera and Gnomes of Ott all coexisting and enjoying the liberal and mercantile policies of the City. Mereskis is a major part within the Elbae Prosperity Sphere and the main trade connection between the Sultanate and Ilrakhan, being one of the core cities within the <a href="Grand Ziflis Company" class="internal-link">G.Z.C.</a> network.</p>
