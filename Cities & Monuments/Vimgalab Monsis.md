<p>The Vimgalab Monsis, literally meaning "Exalted Height" in old Pagri, is a <a href="Zuhurism" class="internal-link">Zuhurist</a> temple and the largest religious complex in the world.</p>
<p>Located on a site measures around 190 hectares within the city of <a href="Nessos" class="internal-link">Nessos</a>, it was constructed between the years 630 and 668 PA, following the first <a href="Zuhiri Holy Wars" class="internal-link">Zuhiri Holy War</a>, and is considered one of the four major holy faith in the classic Zuhuri tradition and often seen as the spiritual centre of the modern faith.</p>
