<p>A minor cursed artefact created by the cevoli Necromancer Phisa Mezr in the middle of the 9th century for her blood bound assassins, the dagger would end up in the possession of the crime lord of <a href="Paper Cities" class="internal-link">Ligoggia</a>, a dwarf known simply as "The Baker" in the 920s. A mercenary of vihri descent by the name Danusz would reportedly win the Dagger of the Baker in 923PA and leave the city behind, how exactly it later found itself in the hands of the goblin Perzuk Uz-Kram is unknown, but he was seen displaying the weapon many times on his travels through Urland, Grevenna and Behera from 924-927PA.</p>
<p>This weapon is said to invoke envy and greed in those whose blood it tastes, while strengthening its wielder with the blood of their victims. </p>
