<p>The relic of a curse put to rest, the Summercrown of Varny was created in the late May of the year 927PA according to the report of Chapter Envoy <a href="Bersi Hurtfing" class="internal-link">Hurtfing</a>. A group of adventurers lifted the curse summoned upon the family Dobry by vanquishing a <a href="Sinlapsi" class="internal-link">Sinlapsi</a> that was haunting the village Apiary, the crown was all that remained of the spirit. Its exact whereabouts are currently unknown.</p>
