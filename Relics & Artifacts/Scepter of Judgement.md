<p>A unique artifact of powerful magicks, the Mace known as the "Scepter of Judgement" is a weapon of renown created during the times of the Heroes of Narav for their Leader, the Paladin Garlav in the early days of the 9th century. Garlav wielded the weapon to great effect when he slew the Beast known as the Selator that had been rampaging through smaller villages and the hillside of the Grachhyll communities, before descending the mountains and making a home within the Sewers of the city of Narav in the south of Halgrach.</p>
<p>The last known bearer of the Mace was the Knight-Errant Ranni Grevil, who went missing during the Summer of 927PA near Wangrad.</p>
