<p>This expertly crafted Dagger is the masterwork of renowned Artificer <a href="Xarvin Jiballo" class="internal-link">Xarvin Jiballo</a>, completed as the final stage of his personal tutelage under legendary Master <a href="Charatov Podlim" class="internal-link">Charatov Podlim</a>. Displaying a complete understanding and practical elegance of the principles of single prism engineering it was under the posession of Jiballo until his capture during the <a href="Standoff at Dimrods Shrine" class="internal-link">Incident at Dimrods Shrine</a> in May 927PA upon which he lost the Dagger. Investigations point towards a Goblin by the name of Quirnux to have committed the theft but the suspect remains at large.</p>
