<p>Yeorite is a a special Ore which disrupts and possibly fully supresses the channelling and steering of Planar Energy in the material realm. It is sometimes referred to as "Null-Rock" or "Anti-Rock" and most commonly used as a smelted down under intense heat and a reducing agent like carbon to transform it into the metal known as Yeollum. Almost all of the Yeorite in the world is found in the Ronra Mountains of far eastern Nirydia and the powerful <a href="Gaal Empire" class="internal-link">Empire of Gaal</a> has managed to keep a near monopoly on the substance.</p>
<p>Yeollum shackles are commonly used in eastern Nirydia as a measure against users of magical powers and have in rare forms even found their way to Ilrakhan and Behera, specifically treasured by members of executive institutions that deal with Mages, such as the <a href="Kherovan Inquisition" class="internal-link">Kherovan Inquisition</a>.</p>
<p>Many have theorized of a connection between the unique suppressive qualities of Yeorite and Yeollum to the effects of the mysterious <a href="Tageyo" class="internal-link">Tageyo Field</a> of southern Behera.</p>
