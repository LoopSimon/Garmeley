<p>This ink is the result of magical experiments conducted by alchemists, infusing the substance with a permanent enchantment spell. It is infused during basic Kynosis with the quality of lingual digestion, making words written in it readable to most anyone who lays their eyes upon it. Reed Ink must be contained in relatively safe conditions and requires a scribe, or any sort of writer using it for that matter, to be in a clear and concise state of mind. Its unique nature lies in the relatively easy process for creating it, concerning planar activation energy. This has made Reed Ink one of the first truly mass-produceable magical concoctions.</p>
<p>It is named after urien Doctor Abathur Reed, the man who invented the process of processing the substance in Uhl, around the year 923PA.</p>
