Cloves:
Native to the tropical and maritime climate of the Kaito and Vinjira coastal regions around the Smaragd Sea.


Cinnamon:
Native to the islands of the Purple Sea and their wet tropical climate.

Pepper:
Native to the Pepper Islands, Drizan and Pervantes, north of the Bay of Kizac and their tropical woody vines and wet climate.
	Propagation: The pepper trade and its popularity within all of Inara has made the good and access to it invaluable.

Ginger:
Native to the Wet Tropical Climates of the Hyktamok, it is mainly found in eastern Nirydia.

Cardamon:
Native to the tropical and maritime climate of the Kaito and Vinjira coastal regions around the Smaragd Sea.

Turmeric:
Native to Sgar Island, north of the Sea of Spores in central Nirydia.

Nutmeg:
Native to the volcanic Qahoris north of Il-Haena
	Propagation: Nutmeg Trees have been transplanted into various islands far away from their original habitat, as the result of elven experiments and the attempt to control the production of the valuable spice. Today the largest production of nutmeg is on the plantations of Myrnifer in the Purple Sea.
