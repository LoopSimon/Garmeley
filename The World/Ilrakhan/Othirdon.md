<p>The Region of Othirdon commonly describes the west of Ilrakhan, beyond  the Kortopi and Urdfall mountains. Coastal cliffs as well as wide sandy plains are both represented in Othirdon and the central parts of the region are dominated by the Liss and Voine River Systems. Commonly Othirdon refers to the area north of the Rogyn Forest but some people include Rogyn itself or even the Duchy of Turpador within it.</p>
<p>The Sertova Peninsular is often considered its own region due to being separated from the mainland by the Virian Sea and Rogyn Fores and all the three areas have distinct cultures and histories, although many cartographers often like to brush Sertova, Rogyn and Othirdon under the same brush</p>
