<p>The Region of Othirdon commonly describes the far west of Ilrakhan, west of the Kortopi and Urdfall mountains. Coastal cliffs as well as wide sandy plains are both represented in Othirdon and the central parts of the region are dominated by the Liss and Voine River Systems. Commonly Othirdon refers to the area north of the Rogyn Forest but some people include Rogyn itself or even the Duchy of Turpador within it.</p>
