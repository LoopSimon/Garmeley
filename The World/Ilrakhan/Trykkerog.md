<p>The Island of Trykkerog lies north of Ilrakhans mainland, and while only connected via the Giants Steps island, generally considered a part of the Ilrakhani continent. It is one of the largest islands in the world and home to the Bkymali peoples, politically dominated by the nations of Baeling, Juelfluk and Vignasoya. </p>
