<p>The Region of Sertova is a diverse Peninsula in the far west of Ilrakhan, connected to the continent via the <a href="Rogyn" class="internal-link">Rogyn</a> Forest. It is bordered in the south by the Lions Bay, named after the <a href="Cevolia" class="internal-link">cevoli</a> sailors who first mapped out the entire peninsula and later settled the Bay itself, in the north by the Wrahi Sea, in the west by the mighty Tilean Ocean and in the east by the Visian Sea, opposite of <a href="Othirdon" class="internal-link">Othirdon</a>. The history of Othirdon and Sertova has been linked ever since the ancient days of the Empire of <a href="Aerlan" class="internal-link">Aerlan</a> and both Regions are connected by not only political and cultural history but also share a common faith as the various <a href="Kalbinism" class="internal-link">Kalbin</a> denominations are the dominant religions of the people in both Sertova and Othirdon.</p>
