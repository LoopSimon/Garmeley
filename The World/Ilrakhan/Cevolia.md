<p>The coastal Region of Cevolia lies south and east of the Kortopis, its many ports sitting on the western Smoking Sea. One of richest regions of the continent in terms of history and surviving cultural traditions, the lands of Cevolia have seen ancient civilisations rise and fall, old gods come, go and come again and many Realms be born and die on her lands.</p>
