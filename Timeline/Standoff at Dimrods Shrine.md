<p>In the later days of May in the year 927PA, a tense situation nearly became a great tragedy when the Mercenaries of the <a href="Laughing Clouds" class="internal-link">Laughing Clouds Band</a> laid siege to the Shrine of Dimrod, within the Mesovo Hills of eastern <a href="Hainach" class="internal-link">Hainach</a>. Reports of the situation are admittedly varied and largely reciprocated by the reani Dimitri Ipros who was part of the conflict until its resolution.</p>
<p>The Shrine of Dimrod, also known as the Eye of the Stonefather, lies around half a days ride south of the Caravans Road within the Hills and is a well maintained location of Worship to the premier deity within reani spiritworship. A variety of smaller and largely isolated villages are located deeper within the hills that tend to be ignored by local authorities and live in a mutual understanding with greater civilization around them, these communities guard and maintain the Shrine and its surrounding paths and have done so for generations. The exact origin for the Shrine as a place of worship is unknown but the almadi Professor <a href="Xarvin Jiballo" class="internal-link">Xarvin Jiballo</a>, himself at the centre of the incident, has theorized it to be over 800 years old and of supernatural making. </p>
