<p>In January of 923PA, the village of Mossbrook within the Deathcap Mire was destroyed due to the appearance of a mysterious Tower over night on the dawn of spring. The exact persons involved, their motivations and their fate remain vastly unknown.</p>
<p>Mossbrook was a village of fishers and eel farmers with a population of between 150-300 people, they were likely  in contact with a few of the other smaller communities within the Deathcap Mire. Records of the settlement it go back to mid  8th century when Miaragua della Vari, member of the <a href="Excavators Guild of Butera" class="internal-link">Buteran Guild of Excavators</a>, described it during an attempt at establishing inland routes to the northern Rakhian Wasteland. Much of the information we know of Mossbrook today is due to the testimony of one of its survivors, the Halfling Velona Morrington, who had been saved out of Mossbrooks ruins by a party of adventuring heroes and brought across the southern Kortopis to the trinvale village of Highslope.</p>
