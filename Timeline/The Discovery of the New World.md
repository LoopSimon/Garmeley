<h2>The Voyage of the Cylla</h2>
<p>The so called "new world" describes the continents of Sirenna and Bricea, south of the Beheran Ocean that were unknown to many of the surface dwelling nations of Inara for thousands of years prior. During the famous "Voyage of the Cylla" Captain Armida Zheni and her crew sailed from the ports of Honshi southward and after an arduous journey found several islands. The islands where inhabited by exotic species of birds that were observed to fly further south, which the sailors correctly explained with the existence of further landmasses. After initial repairs on the Island, dubbed "Tiffins Point" in the honour of a lost crewmate, the Cylla ventured further and eventually discovered the continent of Sirenna in the year 924PA.</p>
<p>Officially sponsored by the Pearl Throne of Honshi, the entire southern continent was quickly proclaimed royal territory upon the return of the Cylla.</p>
