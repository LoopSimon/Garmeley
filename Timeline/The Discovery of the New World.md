<p>The so called "new world" describes the continents of <a href="Sirenna" class="internal-link">Sirenna</a> and <a href="Bricea" class="internal-link">Bricea</a>, south of the Beheran Ocean that were unknown to many of the surface dwelling nations of Inara for thousands of years prior.</p>
<h2>The Voyage of the Cylla</h2>
<p>During the famous "Voyage of the Cylla" Captain Armida Zheni and her crew sailed from the <a href="Hanshi" class="internal-link">hanshi</a>port of Gyedo southward in the March of 923PA and after an arduous journey found several islands. The islands where inhabited by exotic species of birds that were observed to fly further south, which the sailors correctly explained with the existence of further landmasses. After initial repairs on the Island, dubbed "Fontera" in the honour of a lost crewmate, the Cylla ventured further and eventually discovered the continent of Sirenna in the year 924PA.</p>
<p>Officially sponsored by the Pearl Throne of Hanshi, the entire southern continent was quickly proclaimed royal territory upon the return of the Cylla.</p>

<h2>Earlier potential Discoveries</h2>
<p>Records of <a href="Golomid" class="internal-link">Golomid</a> contact with the natives of the Gulf of Sirenna go back almost 400 years prior, to foundation of the Reef Empire.</p>
<p>Infamously, the orcish cartography from merchant enterprises in the far away Sunborn city of <a href="Raximan" class="internal-link">Raximan</a> show sea routes and landmasses southward beyond the Niryd Ocean, and it is likely that the often disparaged and ignored seafaring orcs were indeed the truly first Inarans to discover the southern Continent.</p>
