<p><a href="Aisligos" class="internal-link">Aisligos</a>, a continent across the North Tirean Ocean, was first discovered by skori sailors from <a href="Vignasoya" class="internal-link">Vignasoya</a> in the 7th century PA, although early settlements were quickly abandoned and the larger scale exploration and cross Tirean colonisation would only begin in the mid to late 9th century. </p>
<p>The idea of riches in a "new world to the west" and soon after trade and opportunities with strange people from a strange land, quickly gave hope and interest to many adventuring spirits and enterprising merchants but more importantly woke the eye of not only powerful trading conglomerates but also the dominant rulers of western Ilrakhan. Many Kings and Queens to the far west of Ilrakhan had long been cut off from the incredibly lucrative nirydian and south beheran trade routes that all arrived in the stormy sea through elvish ports and saw a path west as a way to break the mercantile hegemony of the sultanate over eastern trade. The discovery of Aisligos and plans to settle and colonise it grew even more important during the "great turn west" of Meadi politics, that saw the ideas of a Meadi Empire of the west influencing and controlling the lands of not only Meadland but potentially also Othirdon grew more and more popular. The original Skori Seafairers of Vignasoya saw their interests quickly taken over and their means outspend, by the vastly richer realms of meadrath, Blijgrat, Lissvalley, the Cevoli powers and a multitude of Sertovi princes. </p>
