Also known as the "Men of Glass", this Guild was founded in the later 7th century in Trazlov.
The group claims arcane knowledge of the physical composition of the world and controls most of the potion trade in Ilrakhan.
Supposedly their founding goal had been to turn lesser metals or other substances into gold through non magical means, however this process never succeeded in any meaningful of provable way.

They are present throughout most of the larger cities in Ilrakhan and even have outposts in Kherova and the Elbae Sultanate acting as an entirely independent organization from the Vihri Kingdom even though their Headquarters are still at the Violet Hall in Trazlov.

An unofficial rivalry between the Guild and the [[The Ottian Syndicate]] exists, mostly on an economic level, as the two institutions occupy a similar niche as far as arcane supplies are concerned and often have to compete within local communities.

While largely a mercantile institution, they can boast about cutting edge knowledge in experimental [[Alchemy]], and have sponsored various, usually fruitless, expeditions into the ruins of Cyraclion.

The Guild follows a strict set of rules that are, for better or worse, habitually broken and "improved upon", specifically following a Dogma called the "Iron Code" that primarily prohibits research on sentient beings, the creation of sentient life or the fundamental change of the body, as well as the Principle of Control that considers a poison or disease only "finished" upon the completion of a corresponding antidote or medicine.

The Men of Glass specifically entertain a variety of Aquisition Agents colloquially known as "Aquis" or "Yelloweyes". They act as enforcers, arms on the ground and clean up crews.
