<p>The Xerental are the commercial "arm" of the the Hold of <a href="Xer-Kol" class="internal-link">Xer-Kol</a>, initially a private Cartel within the hold that controlled a large portion of the export business with Vihr and Urland, the Xerental eventually grew so big and influential it was subsumed by the official Hold administration and is currently essentially the ministry for trade and finance. The Symbol of the Xerental is the Basalt Sun of <a href="Terem-Zal" class="internal-link">Xer-Higrid</a>, a white Sun with six flares emitting from it.</p>
<p>The business side is commonly associated with Xer-Koli engineering, the holds most famous international export that is only exceeded by the wonders of <a href="Ott" class="internal-link">Ott</a> on the continent, although the Xerental itself of course does not necessarily participate significantly in the design and production aspects of its unique exports. They are widely considered innovators in the realm of firearms and mechanized mining equipment.</p>
<p>One of the largest competitors to the <a href="Trazlov Consortium" class="internal-link">Trazlov Consortium</a>, although at times they have been known to cooperate in ventures. The organisations significant advantages include the control over the Xer-Kol tunnel, a major connection through the Trollback mountains, as well as their significant metalworking expertise</p>
