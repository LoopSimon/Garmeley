<p>The Trazlov Consortium for Free Trade, commonly just called the Consortrium is a commercial and defensive network of merchant guilds and trading ventures in the Torn Sea, that grew from Trazlov in the 7th century PA.</p>
<p>The Consortium expanded in the following 250 years across over a dozen cities and emerged as the dominant mercantile power in the eastern Torn Sea rivalling cities such as Uhl, Schipwig and Sallesand, the major dominant Kontors of the Consortium outside of Trazlov are in Jallbreg, Tversby, Alsebyn, Nykmaes and Voblag. Inland trade in all of Vihr is largely dominated or influenced by Consotrium Guilds as well, outposts in Tlokyemy, Fairriver and even Kisnau are present in the modern day.</p>
<p>The Consortium has vehemently tried to remain apolitical in conflicts such as the great Eastern War and employs agents known as Fingers to protect their interests. In the Year 927PA the Consortiums Headman is Jan Pisudski the owner of the Pisudksi shipping company based in Trazlov itself. </p>
