<p>The Lumin Association of Truth, commonly shortened to Luminists is an international group of former members of Academia that reject what they refer to as "backward and closeminded" censor of magical research. Fundamentally they openly disavow the Wermian Accords and the Orenburg Pact, as well as other such treaties of political authorities, their main principles include religious scepticism and racial tolerance, as a group they have grown in number steadily since the dawn of the 10th century.</p>
