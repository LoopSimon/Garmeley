<p>The Lumin Association of Truth, commonly shortened to Luminists is an international group of former members of Academia that reject what they refer to as "backward and closeminded" censor of magical research. Fundamentally they openly disavow the <a href="Wermian Accords" class="internal-link">Wermian Accords</a> and the <a href="Orenburg Pact" class="internal-link">Orenburg Pact</a>, as well as other such treaties of political authorities, their main principles include religious scepticism and racial tolerance, as a group they have grown in number steadily since the dawn of the 10th century. Made prominent during the denouncement of the infamous cevolian Arcanist <a href="Viccolo di Pora" class="internal-link">Viccolo di Pora</a>, it has been subject to a growing amount of conspiracy theories, ranging from the typical occultist accusations of demonic or necromantic summoning to more political projections like the idea of an international academic cabal of the elite.</p>
