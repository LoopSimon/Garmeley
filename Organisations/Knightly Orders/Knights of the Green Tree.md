<p>This Othiri military organisation was formed in the aftermath of large scale border conflicts with Meadrath in the 8th century BA, it seeks to unite the people of the west against the growing influence of Meadi Imperialism and is less of a religious and more of a cultural institution. The Knights are based in the Fortress of Cherelais in southern <a href="Cimfon" class="internal-link">Cimfon</a> and have recently come under criticism for causing conflicts and distress with Halfling Cities in the <a href="Freelands" class="internal-link">Freelands</a>.</p>
