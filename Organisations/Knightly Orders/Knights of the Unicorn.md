<p>Founded by the eponymous "Unicorn Knight" the <a href="Gethric Saints" class="internal-link">Saint</a> Andre of Trydlew this prestigious and influential order is deeply entrenched within the vihri aristocracy and few members of their rank hail from anywhere else.</p>

<p>Their adherents tend to include dignitaries, higher and lesser nobility that uphold vihrmen tradition and purist gethric values, classically members of the royal family and the court tend to be prominent members of this Organization. As implied by the name, the symbol of the Order is the Unicorn, Vihrs national animal and a majority of its knights ride the majestic animal into battle or on parades</p>

<p>The Orders Headquarter still remains at the outskirts of Trydlew at the Fortress of Madzar and acts as a para military base of its own, although strictly devoted to the Vihri Throne by name. In fact the organisation has recently caused large uproar when they committed their substantial finances and elite forces to the <a href="Garmian Succession Crisis" class="internal-link">Garmian Civil War</a>, following the call to arms by Prince Jakub of Ralpia, 2nd in line to the Throne of Vihr and infamous member of the Knights.</p>
