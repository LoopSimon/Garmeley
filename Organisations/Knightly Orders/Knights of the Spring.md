<p>A mythical group of Knights that were seemingly destroyed during the second <a href="Wars of Elbae Aggression" class="internal-link">Great Elven War</a>, they were Reani and Garmian Knights said to slay foul beasts in the name of the Gethric Candle Flame, mainly operating in the lands around the Stormy Sea. Although they are essentially extinct and heroes of myth, they continue to be emulated and referenced by adventurers, groups and others in the current day, prime examples being <a href="Mikael Bivaldi" class="internal-link">Sir Mikael Bivaldi</a>, the so called "last Knight of the Spring, or the mysterious <a href="Flower Knights" class="internal-link">Flower Knights</a>, a group of Knights seemingly at least aesthetically emulating the image of the Knights of the Spring.</p>
