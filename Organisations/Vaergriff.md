<p>The Vaergriff, also known as "The Vaergriff Secretary" is an organisation within <a href="Vihr" class="internal-link">Vihr</a> that acts as a direct agency of the Ras and the Vaer of Vihr, the full name of the institutions i the "Biuro sekretarzy królewskich Vaergriff na rzecz pokoju i dobrobytu" roughly translating from vihri as "The royal secretaries office of the Vaergriff for peace and prosperity". In essence it acts as a internal and external intelligence service with agents reporting outside of any other hierarchy of power directly to the Crown and the office of the Vaer. </p>
