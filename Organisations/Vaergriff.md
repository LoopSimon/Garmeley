<p>The Vaergriff, also known as "The Vaergriff Secretariat" is an organisation within <a href="Vihr" class="internal-link">Vihr</a> that acts as a direct agency of the Ras and the Vaer of Vihr, the full name of the institutions i the "Biuro sekretarzy królewskich Vaergriff na rzecz pokoju i dobrobytu" roughly translating from vihri as "The royal secretaries office of the Vaergriff for peace and prosperity". In essence it acts as a internal and external intelligence service with agents reporting outside of any other hierarchy of power directly to the Crown and the office of the Sekretar. </p>
<p>The Secretariat acts often veiled in shadows and without much announcements and holds significant authority in the borders of the Rascy. In many ways it is a tool for control and oppression of the Crown in regards to both the people of Vihr and its nobility, as well as the ever powerful and influential clergy. The Vaergriff was likely founded during the time of Ras Miskomir II in the early 8th century.</p>
<p>The current Head of the Vaergriff is the Sekretar of the Realm <a href="Tadeuz Pilsud" class="internal-link">Tadeuz Pilsud</a> who has become one of the most influential and powerful people in Vihri politics, due to his close relationship with Ras <a href="Vormil III" class="internal-link">Vormil III</a>.</p>
