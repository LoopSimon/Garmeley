<p>The Laughing Clouds are a beheran Mercenary Band, currently under employment by the <a href="Kaspar Vasser" class="internal-link">Vasser</a> Cartel, they were founded in 866PA during the Height of the <a href="White Wars " class="internal-link">White Wars</a>.</p>
<p>Currently lead by the imposing Captain <a href="Hector Razzan" class="internal-link">Razzan</a>, the Laughing Clouds are a small outfit of hardly over fifteen members that specializes in small scale extraction, security and bounty hunting. They are named after Captain Irguan ibn Kor, nicknamed "The Laughing Cloud" who formed the group out of Zipivi and Beheran survivors of the bloody battle of the Obsidian Fist, an engagement during the White wars lost by the Kingdom of Tusul.</p>

<p>The Laughing Clouds work mainly jobs within the northern Sultanate and have no real home base of operations, although tend to recently gravitate back to Mereskis where the Vasser Cartel has its Headquarters. As a smaller group of more tight-knight fighters and locals aware of beheri customs and routes, they found a lucrative home within the greater Vasser organization.</p>
