Behera is one of the three major continents on the [[Inara]]n continental area, it borders Ilrakhan at the Cloudspires in the north.
Behera is named after the Beheran Desert in its north, the largest hot desert in the known world, who's name stems from the Ancient Elven "Behiril" meaning "desert".
Traditionally the large island of Il-Haena is viewed as part of Behera.
The largest mountain range on Inara are the Cloudscratchers, and its largest river is the Pralavi.
