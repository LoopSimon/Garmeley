The ancient faith of the people of before the majority of the islands population converted to the Gethric faith.
Bykmali Paganism, as it is described by most contemporary academics, describes the various cults worshipped by the people of Trykkerog before the majority of the islands population converted to the Gethric faith this was the primary faith on the island but it is now a minority religious group, only practiced to a large part in the eastern parts of the island on [[Vignasoya]].
