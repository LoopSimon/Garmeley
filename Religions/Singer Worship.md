<p>Believers in the Singer, usually referring to themselves as the Listeners, believe in a saviour like deity called the Singer that is attempting to wake them from their slumber and collective dreaming. Listeners belief that through various means, one can come closer to hearing the Singers voice and eventually be united with him in the true world where no one suffers and eternal peace lies. Importantly however, to die before being united with the Singer forever stops one from attaining this beautiful life, making death for a Listener more horrifying than simply meaningless. </p>
