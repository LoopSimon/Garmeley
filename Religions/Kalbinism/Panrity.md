<p>Panrity is a branch of <a href="Kalbinism" class="internal-link">Kalbinism</a> advocating for a more interpretive approach to the religious writings of Saint Kalbin. Panrists follow not only the teachings of Saint Kalbin but also those of Panvisa whom they consider a second Saint within the faith of the Creator and the successor of Kalbin himself. Panrity is the predominant religion within the othiri nations of <a href="Tintarol" class="internal-link">Tintarol</a>, <a href="Lissvalley" class="internal-link">Lissvalley</a> and <a href="Cimfon" class="internal-link">Cimfon</a> and has a sizeable following within western <a href="Meadrath" class="internal-link">Meadrath</a> although <a href="Trinicism" class="internal-link">trinic</a> lords have actively persecuted them in the past. It is the nominally largest of the three branches of Kalbinism.</p>
<p>Panrity includes many spirit cult traditions within its wider teachings, considering all fey and inhabitants of the spiritual Realm to be children of the Creator and part of his expression, not to be feared but to be saved. A core characteristic of Panrity is its emphasis on curiosity and self-reflection.</p>
