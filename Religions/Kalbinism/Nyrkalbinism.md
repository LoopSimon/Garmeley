<p>
Nyrkalbinism, sometimes known as Nyrism or Purism, is a branch of <a href="Kalbinism" class="internal-link">Kalbinism</a> that diverges from mainstream Kalbinism with its advocacy for purity and self realization, promoting a life where followers commit themselves for their entire life to a single endeavour, often being associated with a "Nyrist Warrior Culture" but in reality stretching across all walks of life promoting individual exceptionalism.</p>
<p>Especially popular with the Sertovi Halflings and Humans of <a href="Nahr" class="internal-link">Nahr</a> and <a href="Revelle" class="internal-link">Revelle</a>, its traditions  contributed to a great amount to their successful rebellion against the massive <a href="Aerlan" class="internal-link">Empire of Aerlan</a> but also lead to the eventual rise in Halfling Exceptionalism and its racist doctrines.</p>
