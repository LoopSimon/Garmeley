<p>Practiced most famously by the <a href="Peruxa" class="internal-link">Peruxa</a> of <a href="Sihilixar" class="internal-link">Sihilixar</a>, this cult worships the moon as a living entity capable of granting powers, curses and boons. Within the State of Sihilixar itself it is practiced as a form of living cult, viewing the Great Sihir as the wife of Volun, who resides on the Moon.</p>
