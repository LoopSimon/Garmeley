Saints of the Church

  

Gethric the Prophet

Domains: Sun, Good

Favoured Weapon: Light Mace

The Founder of the Faith and Hero of Iztaw, worshiped as the True Prophet and given a higher reverence than even the other saints of the Church.

  
Volboris the Healer

Domains: Law, Healing

Favoured Weapon: Morningstar

A friend and companion of  Gethric in life, Volboris founded the Iztaw Clergy and the Church as an Institution.

  

Fredric Foehammer  
Domains: Glory, War

Favoured Weapon: Warhammer

The Hero of Danipol and the first War of Elbae Aggression, sanctified after his death on the Battlefield and worshipped by Warriors.

  

Rodymir of Voblag

Domains: Death, Knowledge

Favoured Weapon: Scythe

A Scholar and companion of Gethric, he compiled the writings and records of the earliest fathers of the Church into the White Book. Largely responsible for the Adoption of the Concept of the Homestead Beyond and an avid student of the soul.

  

Tronos of the Veil:

Domains: Darkness, Chaos

Favoured Weapon: Quarterstaff

A now controversial Saint, this urien Arcanist sought to commune with Spirits, engaging in questionable methods of Conjuration and Necromancy, his research lead to many discoveries in the Arcane and Divine but was also a major reason for the Wermian Accords and their restriction of certain magical practices.

His worship is rejected by the Kherovan Patriarchat.

  

Dorian the Kind

Domains: Charm, Plant

Favoured Weapon: Halberd

A famous human Knight of the Silver Wheel, Dorian popularized knightly vows as Gethrian Oaths and was responsible for the later dominance and rise of the Order.

  

Markur of the many Eyes:

Domains: Magic

Favoured Weapon: Rapier

Scion of the Korsfall Academy, this Wizard traveled Meadrath and Ur during the aftermath of the War of Trout and Drake and founded many abbeys and schools, helping the commoners and rebuilding the land with Arcane and Divine Magics, he is largely seen as the “ideal Gethrian Wizard”, but rejected by the Kherovan Canon for his free abuse of Arcane Magic.

  

Vazena of Kairos

Domains: Earth, Luck, Artifice

Favoured Weapon: Greataxe

This Kherovan Dwarf was the first Saint of her kind to be canonized, she wrote the Codex Vazenis, unifying many of the natural and spiritual beliefs of pre-Gethrian societies and dwarven/gnome-kind. Credited with the conversion of the Dwarves of Tizin-Kol and the southern Mistspires, as well as the popularization of craftsmanship as a way to achieve Gethrian Ideals, she is well loved by Smiths, Artists, and the like.

  

Melmer Wave-Kissed:

Domains: Water, Travel, 

Favoured Weapon: Trident

Melmer of Uhl, set out on the six voyages to Trykkerog, converting a large part of the population to the Gethrian Faith and founding the city of Malmshamn.

  
  
  
  
  
  

Kifrir the Mirrordwarf: 

Domains: Fire, Evil, Trickery

Favoured Weapon: Dwarven Ram-Hammer

A Dwarf of Tizin-Kol, who earned infamy in the Hobgoblin Wars of the 7th Century.

His application of divine spells in combat has led to some concerns among the more pacifist branches of the Clergy; he is however still worshipped in all three major denominations.

  

Andre of Trydlew:

Domains: Nobility, Animal

Favoured Weapon: Lance

The Count of Trydlew, known as the “Unicorn Knight”, is perhaps the only canonized saint more controversial than Tronos of the Veil.  
A devout Gethrian, he was also a stern loyalist of the Vihri Throne and General during the War with Meadrath that is now known as the War of Horn and Fang.

He is well liked by autocrats and lords, but rejected for various reasons by the Trinicists.

  

Kasimir the Martyr:

Domains: Weather, Community

Favoured Weapon: Flail

Kasimir, a vihri knight, led the great Itharian Expedition in 602 PA, that ended in his death at the hands of the Satyrs of eastern Cyraclion.

  
  

Lyandra of Fairriver

Domains: Strength, Repose  
Favoured Weapon: Greatsword

A Knight of the Silver Wheel, Lyandra was an advocate for Peace during the Garmeley-Vihri War of 648PA and founded the Abbeytown of Fairriver as a haven for those lost and displaced after times of War.

She is worshipped and loved by Commonfolk and is the most popular Saint in Grevenna.

  

Yahrwick of Zeeport

Domains: Liberation, Air

Favoured Weapon: Cutlass

The only Saint canonized by the Bishop of Korsfall, the informal head of the Trinicist Faith, Yahrwick defeated the Trykkerogi Reaverlords and joined Zeeport to the Meadrathi Kingdom.

He is rejected by the other two Gethric Denominations.

  

Ralond Scaleslayer 

Domains: Madness / Truth

Favoured Weapon: Rhoka

A Kherovan Hedge-Knight of the mid 5th century, he is the main character in the infamous Kherovan poem “Ralond and Reana”. His pilgrimage and adventure deep into the Kobold ridden lands of Nirydia and the desolation of Thamiras Rest are historical and accounted for in the poem.  
He despises lies and his silver Rhoka Vulgslasher was said to burn bright when malicious deceit was told around him.
