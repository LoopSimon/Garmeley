<p>Saint Yahrwick of Zeeport represents Liberation and Air within the Gethric Canon, his favoured Weapon is the Cutlass.</p>
<p>The so far only Saint canonized by the Bishop of Korsfall, the informal head of the Trinicist Faith, Yahrwick was a vigilante and "holy" pirate, who gained renown for his defense of the Reaver Invasion of Zeeport. He is rejected by the other two Gethric Denominations.</p>
