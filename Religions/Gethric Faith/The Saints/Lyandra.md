<p>Saint Lyandra of Fairriver represents Strength and Repose within the Gethric Canon, her favoured Weapon is the Greatsword.</p>
<p>A Knight of the Silver Wheel, Lyandra was an advocate for Peace during the Garmeley-Vihri War of 648PA, also known as the Eight Years War, and founded the Abbeytown of Fairriver as a haven for those lost and displaced after times of War. She is worshipped and loved by Commonfolk and is one of the most popular Saint within all denominations. Lyandra is said to have possessed multiple artefacts that are now deemed holy relics in her life, chief among them the Shawl of Respite, the White Blade and the Cuirass of Mercy.</p>
<p>A key aspect Lyandra represents is Strength in mercy and salvation, and the power of staying ones hand when in a position of power. Her Fury is righteous because she offers repose and forgiveness when apt, drawing power from the innocent of past and future that she has sworn to protect. Lyandra welcomes men and women from all paths of life if they are willing to use their might for good, but she does not fret from burning away those who would cast the weak into darkness.</p>
<p>Lyandra was born around 627PA in northern Vihr and died at Fairriver in the year 691PA, her remains were burned and her ashes are kept within a well guarded shrine in the mountains near Fairriver. She was declared a Saint by High-Bisop Taromir III in the Fall of 691PA.</p>
