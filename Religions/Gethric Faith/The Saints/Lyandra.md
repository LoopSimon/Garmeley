<p>Saint Lyandra of Fairriver represents Strength and Repose within the Gethric Canon, her favoured Weapon is the Greatsword.</p>
<p>A Knight of the Silver Wheel, Lyandra was an advocate for Peace during the Garmeley-Vihri War of 648PA, also known as the Eight Years War, and founded the Abbeytown of Fairriver as a haven for those lost and displaced after times of War. She is worshipped and loved by Commonfolk and is one of the most popular Saint within all denominations. Lyandra is said to have possessed multiple artifacts that are now deemed holy relics in her life, chief among them the Shawl of Respite, the White Blade and the Cuirass of Mercy.</p>
