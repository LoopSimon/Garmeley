<p>Saint Palus the Gnome-Friend represents Runes and Erosion within the Gethric Canon, his favoured Weapon is the Gladius.</p>
<p>An Urien Scholar and priest that studied with Gnomish communities, while primarily a theologian interested in the exchange of spiritualism between human and gnomish culture, he opened a wider door for arcanists and academics within their society, especially favoured in the Trinicist Regions.</p>
