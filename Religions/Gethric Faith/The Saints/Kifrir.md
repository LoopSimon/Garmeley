<p>Saint Kifrir the Mirror-Dwarf represents Trickery and Evil within the Gethric Canon, his favoured Weapon is the Ram-Hammer.</p>
<p>A Dwarf of Tizin-Kol, who earned infamy in the Hobgoblin Wars of the 7th Century. His application of divine spells in combat has led to some concerns among the more pacifist branches of the Clergy; he is however still worshipped in all three major denominations.</p>
