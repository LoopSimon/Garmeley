<p>Gethric the Prophet represents Fire and Good within the Gethric Canon, his favoured Weapon is the Mace.</p>
<p>The Founder of the Faith and Hero of Iztaw, worshiped as the True Prophet and given a higher reverence than even the other saints of the Church. Gethric is said to have been born in Wermia, but is often portrayed as a vihri farmer in clerical art.</p>
