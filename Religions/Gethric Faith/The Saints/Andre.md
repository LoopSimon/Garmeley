<p>Saint Andre of Trydlew represents Nobility and Animals within the Gethric Canon, his favoured weapon is the Lance.</p>
<p>The Count of Trydlew, known as the “Unicorn Knight”, is perhaps the only canonized saint more controversial than Tronos of the Veil. A devout Gethrian, he was also a stern loyalist of the Vihri Throne and General during the War with Meadrath that is now known as the War of Horn and Fang. He is well liked by autocrats and lords, but rejected for various reasons by the Trinicists.</p>
