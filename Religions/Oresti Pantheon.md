<p>The ancient oresti pantheon, preceding even the Rakhian Invasion, was the dominant religion in the ancient <a href="Orestis" class="internal-link">oresti</a> Civilization, a great culture of old <a href="Cevolia" class="internal-link">Cevolia</a>, preceding the eponymous Kingdom of <a href="Cevolis" class="internal-link">Cevolis</a>.
The Divine female was the important axis of the pantheon, with the three Goddess of Tirea, Gizana and Servila being the primary deities of the Faith.</p>

The Cevolian Pantheon consisted of the following deities:

Tirea, the goddess of Harvest, see [[Cult of Tirea]]
Gizana, the goddess of Sleep
Servila, the goddess of Fortune

Ilos, God of Strength, Husband Servila
Perix, God of the Sea, Husband of Tirea
Goros, God of Magic, Husband of Servila
