The ancient oresti pantheon, preceding even the Rakhian Invasion, was the dominant religion in the ancient [[Orestis]] Civilization, a great culture within Cevolis, preceeding the eponymous Kingdom of Cevolis.
The Divine female was the important axis of the pantheon, with the three Goddess of Tirea, Gizana and Servila being the primary deities of the Faith.

The Cevolian Pantheon consisted of the following deities:

Tirea, the goddess of Harvest, see [[Cult of Tirea]]
Gizana, the goddess of Sleep
Servila, the goddess of Fortune

Ilos, God of Strength, Husband Servila
Perix, God of the Sea, Husband of Tirea
Goros, God of Magic, Husband of Servila
