<p>Bigronism is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices of disciplines that originated in modern day <a href="Jorvistan" class="internal-link">Jorvistan</a>, aimed at controlling body and mind to attain the liberation of ones soul from its earthly tethers. During the Jorvi Freedom Wars of the 6th century PA the ban of Bigronism caused a military war of independence with the dominant <a href="Twintail Imperium" class="internal-link">Twintail Imperators</a>, martial schools and militant aspects have grown more dominant within the Philosophy ever since.</p>
