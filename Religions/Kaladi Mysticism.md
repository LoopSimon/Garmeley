The Kaladi are divine spirits that were once mortal and deified after death, usually heroes and leaders of the [[Hobgoblin]] people. They are honoured in festivals and associated with certain types of rhythms and sounds.
Often Kaladi are associated with geographic locations, like caves,  mountains, rivers or cities notably not as personifications of nature, like within the Faith of the [[Kindly Lords]] for examples, but as patrons and patronesses.

Popular Kaladi include:

1. Barxu, 
	the kaladi associated with sexuality, fertiliy, water and love. 
	It is said Barxu was a wise shaman in her life that led her people to the Green Grotto, named after her.

2. Tirbo
