Also known as the "Ratfolk", a derogatory term usually used to insult them, the Shirin of Nirydia are the most numerous race on the continent, having ruled the [[Twintail Empire]] for houndreds of years.
They are rodent-like humanoids that once were native to the subterranean underlands, averaging at a height between 4 and 5 feet with extremely pronounced noses and tails often covered in varying degrees of fur.
