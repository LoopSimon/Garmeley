<p> Also known as the "Ratfolk", a derogatory term usually used to insult them, the Shirin of Nirydia are the most numerous race on the continent, having ruled the <a href="Twintail Imperium" class="internal-link">Twintail Imperium</a> for hundreds of years.</p>
<p>They are rodent-like humanoids that once were native to the subterranean underlands, averaging at a height between 4 and 5 feet with extremely pronounced noses and tails often covered in varying degrees of fur. They vastly prefer low elevation regions as their weaker lungs do not allow them to breath the thin air in mountainous or even hilly regions.</p>
<p>The Tails of the Shirin are one of their most identifying features and possess thermoregulatory qualities: the high muscular and connective tissue densities of the tail, along with ample muscle attachment sites along its plentiful caudal vertebrae, facilitate specific proprioceptive senses to help orient the shirin in a three-dimensional environment even without any vision.</p>
