<p>The Zheguan is a species native to the beheran desert, somewhat resembling rays. They lie in wait, often largely covered by sand in the desert dunes. Their diet is mixed and they tend to feed on both small desert organisms or worms but also rely on opportunistic hunting patterns, employing their stinger to great effect.</p>
<p>Contact with the spinal blade of the stinger causes largely local trauma due to the incision but perhaps more dangerous is the venomous nature of the Zheguan Sting. The Zheguan Venom is known by the Yorgosi, Beheri or other cultures within desert as a fast acting agent causing first local and then total necrosis of the flesh. </p>
