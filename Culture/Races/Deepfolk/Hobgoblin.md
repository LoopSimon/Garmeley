Even though their Urien Name alludes to it, these creatures have little in common with their Goblinoid "Cousins", and it is scientifically unconfirmed if the two are actually genetically closely related.

Burly, lanky and muscled, Hobgoblins stand at around an average of 2 metres, towering over most fellow residents of the [[Underlands]], they tend to grow up to 75 years old and posess an average aptitude for magick.

Hobgoblins, also known as Dirgae in their own language, tend to reside in large caverns near the surface, preferring wet and colder climates. They are avid hunters and usually organize in smaller communities instead of larger cities.
They have been known to organize in large warbands under strong leaders but there are few examples of continuous nation states under hogboblin majority.
