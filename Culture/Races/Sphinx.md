<p>Sphinxes, sometimes referred to as Sphinges or Synxi, are creatures of unknown origin with the head of a human, the body of a lion and the wings of an eagle, they are primarily found on the northern Beheran continent. Sphinxes are intelligent creatures capable of channelling planar energies as magic, they are religiously revered within <a href="Elven Paragonworship" class="internal-link">elven Paragonworship</a>  and sometimes guard ancient Tombs of Pharaos of the past.</p>
