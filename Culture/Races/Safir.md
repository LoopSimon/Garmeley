<p>Safir is a catch all term describing the various subraces of reptiloid humanoids inhabitaing the southern Jungles of <a href="Behera" class="internal-link">Behera</a>. Many Safir exhibit Serpentine characteristics, but it is a common misconception to ascribe these on all members of the species when it is truly more of a marker of the populous pravi safir.</p>
<h2>Pravi Safir</h2>
<p>Even amongst the Pravi, these safir are prone to sharp differences in appearance scale colours are usually varying between warm colours such as browns, yellows and reds and elaborate patterns mark genetic heritage amongst the pravi. Notably pravi give off almost no scent a mere human could detect, although those of keener senses often associate them with a dry, musty odor.</p>
