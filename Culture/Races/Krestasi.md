<p>The term Krestasi stems from the Qari word Krest, meaning literally "Crust" and refers to the various species of Crustaecean within the Oceans of the World.
Since originally coined by Qari Sailors it is primarily used to identify the various inhabitants of the <a href="Golomid" class="internal-link">Golomid Empire</a>.</p>
