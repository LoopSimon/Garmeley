Generally smaller sized, Kobolds are bipedal, scaled reptilian humanoids that tend to weigh about 120kg, standing at around 2 metres. They are native to the [[Niryd Steppe]] and show variance in phenotype based on their environment: There are reports for significant differing in size, head- and tail shape based on regional origin, as well as the striking change in the tint of their scale colour.
Kobolds are abnormally prone to mutation and worship stronger and more exotic variants among them religiously and culturally, this can take on extremes such as some Kobolds standing at almost 9 metres tall and possessing 2 or even 3 heads and other abnormal characteristics.
Although they like claim kinship to [[Dragon]]s, there is no evidence outside of the mythological that would agree with the idea the two races are related. 
Many Kobolds of the Steppes have evolved to posess extremly durable protective hides covering their entire body, to endure the dangerous Aspira Bladegrass.
