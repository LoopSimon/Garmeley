A general term describing the various contemporary and / or historical named entities of the [[Spiritual Realm]] that have occasionally even taken form on the [[Material Realm]].
Generally speaking these are  found as embodiments of natural phenomena, often being objects of Worship in faiths such as the [[Kindly Lords]] or the [[Terem-Zal]] but the term is often used as a catch-all for the sentient or especially powerful creatures born within the Spiritual Realm.
