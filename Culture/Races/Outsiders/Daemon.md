*Grahul the Chained*
	Bound by the Elf Alparaz in the 7th century BA within the [[Material Realm]].
	Though its existence was revealed to the greater world later on, it has been officially refuted by the Sultanate that the entity still resides on our Plane.

Thalkil Twice-born
	This creature is often assumed to be of daemonic nature and was supposedly slain by the Locni Three, a group of adventurers travelling the east coast of Ilrakhan in the 7th century PA.
	Rumours of his ties to Cults of [[Augurism]] and supposed experimentation on his physical remains have been largely disproven as conspiracy theories.
