<p>The Term Fey is a general catch-all term describing all manner of creatures present within the spiritual and material realm at the same time.</p>

<h2>Hags</h2>
<p>A Hag is a supernatural creature, that results of the Ascendence within a Circle of shamanistic practitioners. If successful a member of such a Circle may absorb the physical and spiritual energy of his fellows and ascend or "descend" into hagdom retaining some of their own physical characteristics but also obtaining some of their victims.</p>
<p>It is important to note that Circles tend to be surprised and betrayed by the Hags actions and usually are originally formed as peaceful and communal groupings to help each other understand their spiritual and magical capabilities.</p>
<p>Hags are incredibly in tune with both the physical and material realm, walking freely between them but being bound to their location within both, often times this results in a Hags permanent residence becoming something of a lair for the creature.</p>
