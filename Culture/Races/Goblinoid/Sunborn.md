The Sunborn are a race of goblinoids vaguely related to their [[Hobgoblin]] and [[Goblinoid]] cousins, that entirely live on the surface of the world. They prefer arid climates, often dwell in the desert or savannahs and are capable of absorbing and retaining a remarkable amount of water due to a protective gel-like film on their skin.

Similar to some surface dwelling Goblins, they often live in smaller nomadic clans and for example roam the sands of Behera in equal communities with wandering Beheran or Yorgosi tribes.
But they are also known to settle and form larger settlements, for example within the city state of [[Raximan]] or the Kingdom of [[Tilduren]] on the Island of Durennis.

Sunborn tend to reach a heigh of around 4ft and grow extremely large and round ears akin to that of desert creatures, their lifespan is ca 90 years.
