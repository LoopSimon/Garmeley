<p>An ancient people, the mutated <a href="Ogre" class="internal-link">Ogre</a> of the Kragi are referred to as Cyclopi: taller, smarter and one-eyed compared to their Tallfolk Cousins, but perhaps more importantly noticeably more keen on civilization building. These mutations are commonly believed to be a consequence of the close proximity of the Kragi to the ruins of Cyraclion, in perhaps a similar fashion as the Satyr Clans across the Strait of Ithar. <br> They contend themselves with observing the Stars from elevated constructions, but reports of wars with Satyr warbands speak to a not entirely pacifist nature.</p>
