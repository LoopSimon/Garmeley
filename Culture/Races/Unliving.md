[[Ahrmiz Saceren]]
%%
<p>A catch all term for those creatures that have died in life and were brought back through various ways and in differing shapes and forms. Usually people refer to those as Unliving that retain a form of sentience and sometimes even self-awareness rather than all organisms brought back. The highest form of Necromancy, the science of wrestling that from beyond that has died in the past, is this perfect reanimation that reverses death and drags body, soul and mind back into the material world.</p>
