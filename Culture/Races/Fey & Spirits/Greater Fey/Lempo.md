<p>The "Father of Moths" Lempo is a Fey that has appeared in Nostroya and Trykkerog. He is associated with deceit, greed and pride as well as an unnatural drive ending in self-destruction. Commonly depicted with moth like imagery and favouring the appearence of a giant moth crature when manifesting. He is almost always viewed in a negative and warning light and is said to have even deceived the Gods themselves.</p>
<p>He appears in many Bkymali and Nostroyan tales, such as the <a href="Nostroya" class="internal-link">story</a> of the Jättviki, the Fall of Rismus or the Deep Slumber. His followers often use magic and trickery.</p>
