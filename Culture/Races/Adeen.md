<p>The Adeen are a race of molluscoids that dwell in high elevations. While varying considerably between sub species, most Adeen possess a large, sack-like body and various sensitive tentacle like arms. They are sentient and intelligent beings capable of forming emotional bonds and building societies. Adeen exist within Inara and Sirenna, usually habiting hilly or mountainous climates. They emit a gaseous substance, usually invisible to humanoid sight that they float in and can use to communicate with by changing pressure and quality of the gas. Adeen grow to a mass of around 50-60 kg when reaching adulthood after 20 years of childhood and tend to live for around 100-120 years. It is often speculated that Adeen posess an innate affinity to what we in modern science describe as <a href="Alchemy" class="internal-link">Kynosis</a> or Alchemy, as dissection and research has not been able to explain the Adeen ability to fluently manipulate their gas.</p>
<p>Adeen rarely breed and are not a populous race, with certain subraces having been hunted to extinction such as the Gweradeen native to the Trollback mountains. They are not known to form larger societies but instead dwell in smaller communities and often exhibit pacifist behaviours, although this is likely not a genetic but rather a cultural trait, concerning the observed examples. Their gas can be dangerous to humanoids, depending on severity even fatal which has led them to be labelled as "monsters and demons" throughout history, only a few cases of permanent exchange are known. The glands that produce the gaseous fumes are highly sought after by alchemists and collectors, coupled with their often misunderstood practices and the little popular knowledge about the Adeen, this has led to many hunts and hostile encounters on at least the Inaran continent.</p>
The two known greater habitations of Adeen on Inara are the <a href="Gladeen" class="internal-link">Gladeen Nest of Ronra</a> in north-eastern Nirydia, as well as the <a href="Vulpus" class="internal-link">Vulpus Spherea</a>, located in Othirdon.
