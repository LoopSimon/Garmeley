***Heximar***
	known as "the Unlit" due to his black scales, descended the central Kortopis in the late 3rd century BA. 
	After devastating the Meadi Highlands he was supposedly slain by the Hero Gismar, 
	founder of the House of Gis that would continue to rule the Gisvale for centuries.

***Fagnir***
	mythical Dragon of the North mentioned in the Saga of Ylva.
