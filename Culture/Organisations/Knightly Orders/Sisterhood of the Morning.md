<p>The Sisterhood of the Morning is a group of "wise women" most commonly associated with spiritual guides, medical healers and blessed women of grace that seeks to embody the virtues of Saint and Church father Volboris. Their founding members date the origins of the group back to the early days of the faith. </p>
