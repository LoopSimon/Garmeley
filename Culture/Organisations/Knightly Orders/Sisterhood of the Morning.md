<p>The Sisterhood of the Morning is a group of "wise women" most commonly associated with spiritual guides, medical healers and blessed women of grace that seeks to embody the virtues of Saint and Church father Volboris. Their founding members date the origins of the group back to the early days of the faith. They are decidedly independent in terms of political association and are also one of the few elder gethric institutions that has largely avoided taking sides during the Schism of Gethricism and Trinicism. </p>
<p>The Sisterhood is an entirely female organization with a well defined hierarchical structure. They operate in similar fashion to Knightly Orders and is organized within regional chapters with the largest one being the <a href="Urbridge" class="internal-link">Urbridge</a> Chapter in central Urland.</p>
