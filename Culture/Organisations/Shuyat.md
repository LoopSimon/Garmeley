[[Elbae]]
%%
<p>Literally meaning "shadow" in ancient elvish, this mysterious group acts as a unit of foreign and domestic information control and acquisition for the <a href="Elbae" class="internal-link">Sultanate</a>. Officially recognized as a cultural remnant of days long gone and more of a traditionally institution, its true purpose and responsibilities are subject to conspiracy theories and mystery.</p><p>Originally founded as a subsect of priests in the 4th century BA, they largely faded into obscurity by the 6th century, although conspiracy theorists largely connect them to operations up until the modern day.</p>
