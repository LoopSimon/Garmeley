<p>Kharnov is a town within the northern Grachwoods Region of Grachy, sitting alongside the Orin River. Before the fall of Roygviil it was known under its pehki name, Breel, but siege and destruction left most of it abandoned following the wars, it has recently enjoyed a larger rebuilding effort and is growing into a prospering town, already threatening to outshine Wangrad, the Regions official capital.</p>
<p>Following the destruction of Breel during the final Roymer-Grachi War of 690PA almost no people remained around the structures of the former town turned roymer military fortress. The premier building left standing was the signature White Tower of Breel, a former place of roymer druidic worship of the aeld and in later years the centre of the military fortifications in the town. The Mage Tybor Leow was granted permission to take up residence within the Tower by Andrej II of Stir, the ruling King of Halgrach at the time in 697PA, seven years after the conclusion of the War and the Fall of Breel.</p>
<p>Leow and his circle were for a long time the only people brave enough to dwell in the ruins and the location became soon known simply as Leow Tower, iconic for his plain white stone rising up from the earth. A few decades later smaller merchant and farming communities began growing again around Leow Tower and reclaimed the land at the Riverbend of the Orin, leading to the foundation of the town of Kharnov officially in 788PA. Following the End of the Kingdom of Halgrach with the Brothers War of the 830s, Kharnov was officially designated as part of the Northern Grachwoods Ushkyn, held at Wangrad in the far east, although it has since then only continued to outgrow the eastern town in size, commerce and popularity.</p>
