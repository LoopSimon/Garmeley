<p>Izevkh is a Grachy city on the bend of the Jaerflow River, sitting beneath the Jaerfall Waterfall and opposite a small cluster of hills and mountains known as the Irdknuckles, it is a major hub of commerce within the more rural east and north of Grachy.</p>
<p>The City is home to the Cathedral of Remembrance, also known as St <a href="Igor" class="internal-link">Igor's </a> Cathedral but usually not called this due to the larger and more important church of the same name in the city of Modva, Izevkh Castle, and the Grey Mausoleum. The Jaerfall makes shipping from further up the river and the areas around Trozk impossible without coming through the land route into the city, similarly the many farmlands around Izevkh supply its northern neighbour Kharnov with valuable produce so the land routes coming in and out of Izevkh have made it a very popular destination for merchant caravans. The City profits from the political influence of its elected Leader, Gram Ruda, who's popular standing within the Traditionalist Faction of the Grand Ushkyn at Halgrach is very helpful in regards to expansionist charters granted by the central authority of the Republic. This has led to an extremely aggressive policy of deforestation and settlement along the northern Jaerflow, as Izevkhs  Boyars have begun to compete with the entrenched and rich Landowners of the West.</p>

<h2>Demographics</h2>
<p>The city is a majority grachy human settlement although humans from vihr and various bykmali traders make up small minorities. The largest minority however are Mistspire dwarves who enjoy great privilege in the city and are even given an honorary seat at the Ushykn of the Region for their contributions to Izevkhs growth and prosperity, many of them stem from the communities around <a href="Tizin-Kol " class="internal-link">Tizin-Kol </a>Tizin-Kol originally but are now a few generations removed from initial migrants.</p>
<h2>Government and Politics</h2>
<p>Like all regional Capitals in the Republic, Izevkh is governed by a Gram elected by the Ushkyn, currently a position held by Eril zi Ruda, an influential and powerful politician on the national stage, proud traditionalist and ambitious leader, aiming to build out the traditionally poorer backwater of eastern Grachy. The Ushkyn of the city convenes in the Orsrag Hall where zi Ruda holds court as well. </p>
