<p>Giolad Crater, often simply referred to as "the Giolad" is a gigantic pit located on the continent of <a href="Sirenna" class="internal-link">Sirenna</a>. It can be found at the southern slopes of the Edami Mountain range with a diameter of approximately 1000 meters and its true depths are unknown, likely being over 15 kilometres deep.</p>
