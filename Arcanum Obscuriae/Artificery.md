<p>Prosum, the gnomish study of artificery covers the following three pillars in classical ottian schooling:</p>
	<p>Tiogum: the Maintenance of an Artifact</p>
	<p>Vellum: the Modulation of an Artifact</p>
	<p>Ixinum: the Creation of an Artifact</p>
<p>Notable an Artifact is a crucial step in the process of casting, also known as the manipulation of planar energies. It enables the creation of a Prism within a Fluxbridge that allows a Caster, or more likely the activating Force of a given spell, to adjust and direct planar energies. A solid grasp of  <a href="Basic Magical Theory" class="internal-link">basic</a> and <a href="Advanced Magical Theory" class="internal-link">advanced</a> magical theory is posited by the following overview of the Art of Prosum.</p>
