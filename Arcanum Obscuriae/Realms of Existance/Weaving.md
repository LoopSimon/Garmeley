<p>The Term Weaving generalizes all applications of realmatic Energy, as opposed to planar energy. </p>
<p>The Existence of Dulmic Gates, is often cited as an example for the possibility of permanent Weaving, connecting the material and spiritual realm permanently, akin to a permanent Fluxbridge.</p>
<p>The most common form of Weaving is the, often shamanistic, practice of "spiritwalking" practiced by certain cultures such as the niryd Kobolds. It is often assumed that weaving, unlike casting, requires certain biological features, but this could be easily discarded as academic conjecture due to cultural traditions. The Art of Weaving is not considered a higher science among the major Institutions of Learning on Ilrakhan, Behera or Nirydia, leading to a simplified and ignorant perspective by the greater contemporary academic consensus on this School of research.</p>
