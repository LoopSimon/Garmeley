The Positive Plane lies on the so called "Divine Axis" opposite the [[The Negative Plane]], Outsiders of this Plane are referred to as [[Celestial]]s.
According to the Tronosic School of Thought within [[Gethricism]], the souls of sentient beings may ascending on the Divine Axis, those with sufficient knowledge of themselves, having lived according to the teachings of self realization and oaths that the Faith teaches, being able to retain individualistic characteristics and persona in the so called "Farmstead Beyond", a romantic name for a theoretical paradisical subplane of the Positive Plane.
